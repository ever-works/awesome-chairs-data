## Ergonomic Gaming Chairs & Setups by Humanscale

### Overview
Humanscale's Gaming Collection introduces a range of ergonomic gaming chairs designed to enhance comfort and performance for gamers during extended sessions. These chairs incorporate ergonomic principles typically found in high-end office seating to support gamers in achieving peak performance.

### Features
- **Adjustable Armrests**: Customize the armrests to your preferred height and angle for optimal comfort.
- **Lumbar Support**: Integrated lumbar support to maintain healthy posture and reduce back strain.
- **Breathable Mesh Backrests**: Provide ventilation to keep you cool and comfortable during long gaming sessions.
- **Sculpted Cushions**: Reduce pressure points and conform to your body for a personalized fit.
- **Pivoting Backrest**: Adapts to the natural movements of your spine, ensuring continuous support.
- **Gel Arm Pads and Seats**: Combat muscle fatigue, offering long-term comfort and support.
- **Optional Headrest**: Provides additional support for the head and neck in various positions.
