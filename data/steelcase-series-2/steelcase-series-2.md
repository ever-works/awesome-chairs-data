# Steelcase Series 2

**Category:** Ergonomic Office Chairs  
**Brand:** Steelcase  
**Slug:** `steelcase-series-2`

A fully adjustable ergonomic task chair designed for people who spend long hours at a computer, with features aimed at supporting a healthy sitting posture.

---

## Features

- **Ergonomic design** for extended computer and desk work
- **Flexor system in the backrest** that adapts to the user’s movements for dynamic back support
- **Adjustable lumbar support** to better align and support the lower back
- **Multiple arm adjustments** (e.g., height/position) to support forearms and shoulders
- **Adjustable seat settings** (e.g., seat height and position) to fit different body sizes and desk setups
- **Posture support–focused construction** to help maintain a comfortable, healthy seated posture during long periods of sitting
