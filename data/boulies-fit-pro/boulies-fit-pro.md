### Overview
The Boulies Fit Pro is a premium, highly adjustable ergonomic chair designed for long hours of computer work or gaming. It offers extensive fit customization—including an adjustable seat depth typically found on high-end models—to accommodate a wide range of body types and sitting postures.

### Features
- 3-level angle backrest recline: 100°, 112.5°, 125°
- Tilt mechanism with position lock
- Built-in adjustable lumbar support
- Integrated adjustable head support
- Adjustable seat depth
- 8-way adjustable armrests
- Designed for extended computer use or gaming sessions
- Suitable for a wide range of body types and postures
