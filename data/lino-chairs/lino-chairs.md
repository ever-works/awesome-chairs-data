# Lino Chairs

- **Brand:** Herman Miller  
- **Category:** Ergonomic Office Chairs  
- **Best for:** People who sit at a desk or computer for long stretches and want ergonomic support at a value-oriented price point.

## Description
Lino is an ergonomic task chair designed to deliver performance seating features—such as adjustable lumbar support and synchronized tilt—in a more value-focused package. It is intended for both workplace and home offices and emphasizes long-term comfort for extended sitting.

## Features
- **Ergonomic design for long sitting sessions**  
  - Designed for people who sit at a desk or computer for extended periods.
- **Total spinal support**  
  - Contoured seat works with the backrest system to support the spine.  
  - High-performance Duo Suspension backrest provides spinal support.  
  - Breathable suspension material for airflow and comfort.  
  - Integrated lumbar support created through varied thread tensions in the suspension.
- **Adjustable lumbar support**  
  - Lumbar support can be adjusted to better fit different users.  
- **Synchronized tilt mechanism**  
  - Supports balanced movement and promotes dynamic sitting.  
  - Aims to maintain comfort and support through posture changes.
- **Performance seating DNA**  
  - Based on Herman Miller’s research and design for performance work chairs.  
  - Intended to provide balanced movement and dynamic fit for different body types.
- **Customizable aesthetics**  
  - Frame color options: Black or Mineral.  
  - Multiple suspension color options (six colors).  
  - Wide range of upholstery options for the seat.  
  - Base options: color-matched to frame or polished aluminum for a more refined look.
- **Design pedigree**  
  - Designed by Sam Hecht and Kim Colin in collaboration with Herman Miller’s ergonomic and performance seating expertise.
