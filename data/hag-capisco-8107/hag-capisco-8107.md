#### Description

The **HAG Capisco 8107** is a distinctive ergonomic chair designed for dynamic sitting. Featuring a saddle seat, this chair encourages movement and comfort, making it an excellent choice for both office and gaming environments. Its unique design was inspired by the active seating posture of a horse rider, promoting balance and core engagement.

#### Features

- **Saddle Seat Design:** Inspired by equestrian seating, offering dynamic support and promoting movement.
- **Adjustable Features:** Customizable seat height, depth, and back height to fit different body types and preferences.
- **Versatile Use:** Suitable for office and gaming settings, supporting various postures.
- **Award-Winning Design:** Recognized with "The Classic Award for Design Excellence" by the Norwegian Design Council in 2001.
- **5-Day Express Shipping:** Available for quick dispatch, ensuring timely delivery.
