# Sitmatic GoodFit High Back Chair

**Overview**

The Sitmatic GoodFit High Back Chair is a versatile seating solution designed to accommodate a broad range of users, from 5’3” to 6’5” and beyond. Crafted with ergonomic precision, this chair is ideal for those seeking comfort and customizable support in their office environment. Originally rooted in Italian furniture design, Sitmatic now manufactures its components in California, ensuring high-quality, robust construction.

**Features**

- **Ergonomic Design**: Engineered for optimal comfort and support, suitable for various body types.
- **High Back**: Provides enhanced back support, ideal for prolonged use.
- **Standard Seat**: Comfortable seating surface designed to accommodate most users.
- **Steel Frame**: Durable construction with "no weight limits" capability.
- **Lifetime Component Warranty**: Assurance of quality and longevity.
- **Customizable Options**: 
  - **Arm Options**: 
    - **+7K**: Adjustable arm height (4” in 10 positions) and width (2” per side), with Soft Gel Caps.
    - **+6K**: Foldable arms with adjustable height (3” in 6 positions), no pivot, with Soft Gel Caps.
  - **Additional Options**: Inflatable Lumbar Bolster, Tailbone Seat Cutout, Memory Foam Seat Topper, optional Seat Slider with OT Mechanism.
