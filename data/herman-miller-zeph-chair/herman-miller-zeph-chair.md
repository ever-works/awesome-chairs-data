## Overview

The Herman Miller Zeph Chair is a compact, design-focused office/computer chair built for all-day use. Instead of multiple adjustment levers, it uses a flexible one-piece shell to support your body, making it a good fit for small workspaces and for people who prefer a simple, low-adjustment seating experience.

## Features

- **One-piece polymer shell** that flexes to support your body rather than relying on numerous mechanical adjustments.
- **Compact footprint** suitable for small or space-constrained work areas.
- **Design-forward aesthetic** intended to complement modern home offices and workspaces.
- **Intended for all-day use**, suitable as an office or computer chair.
- **Minimal adjustment approach**, reducing the need to fine-tune multiple levers and controls.
- **Optional 3D-knit seat pad** for added comfort and visual texture.
- **Optional arms** for users who prefer arm support.
- **High weight capacity** supporting users up to **350 lb (158.8 kg)**.
- **Broad seat-height adjustment range** of **14.8"–20.3"** to fit a variety of users and desk heights.
- **Long-term durability coverage** with a **12-year warranty** (manufacturer warranty).
