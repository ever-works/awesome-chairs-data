# Herman Miller Office Chairs

**Category:** Ergonomic Office Chairs  
**Brand:** Herman Miller  
**Brand Logo:** ![Herman Miller](https://www.hermanmiller.com/content/dam/hmicom/page_assets/homepage/primary-nav/herman-miller-logo.svg)

A range of ergonomic office chairs designed for extended computer work in home offices and professional workstations. The collection includes multiple high‑performance models (such as Aeron, Embody, Cosm, and Mirra 2) focused on posture support, adjustability, and all‑day comfort.

---

## Features

### General Collection Features
- Ergonomic designs intended for long hours of seated computer work
- Office chairs suitable for both home offices and professional workplaces
- Focus on posture support for spinal alignment and healthy sitting
- High level of adjustability (varies by model) to fit different users and tasks
- Options aimed at all‑day comfort during intensive work sessions
- Collection of “performance seating” models that can be compared and configured online

### Example Models in the Collection

**Aeron Chair**
- Ergonomic office chair with distinctive ergonomic back support
- 5‑star base for stability and mobility
- Designed to provide support for prolonged computer and desk work

**Embody Chair**
- Ergonomic office chair (example shown in blue finish)
- Designed for high‑performance seating and extended sitting periods

**Cosm Chair**
- Available in a high‑back version for increased upper‑back support
- Example configuration shown with leaf arms
- Offered in finishes such as Glacier light blue

**Mirra 2 Chair**
- Office chair with Butterfly back design
- Shown in alpine colorway with a silver alloy base
- Designed for ergonomic performance and flexible back support
