# Razer Enki X

**Category:** Others  
**Brand:** Razer  
**Product type:** PC / gaming chair for long computer and gaming sessions

Razer Enki X is the more affordable, essential version of the Razer Enki gaming chair. It focuses on core ergonomic and comfort features designed for all‑day use and long gaming sessions, with optimized weight distribution and a wide seat base.

## Features

### Ergonomics & Comfort
- Designed for **all‑day comfort** during long computer and gaming sessions.
- **Optimized weight distribution** to reduce pressure points over extended use.
- **110° extended shoulder arches** to better support upper body and shoulders.
- **21" ultrawide seat base** to accommodate a wide range of body types and postures.
- **Built‑in lumbar arch** (inferred from section heading) to support the natural curve of the lower back and encourage healthier sitting posture.

### Product Positioning
- **Essential, more affordable variant** of the Razer Enki chair.
