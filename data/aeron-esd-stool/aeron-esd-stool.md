## Aeron ESD Stool

**Brand:** Herman Miller  
**Category:** Others (ESD Seating / Stool)  
**Slug:** `aeron-esd-stool`

A high-seat, ESD-safe version of Herman Miller’s Aeron stool, designed for technical workstations and sit-stand desks where electrostatic discharge protection and ergonomic support are required for long computer sessions.

### Features
- **ESD-safe construction**
  - Designed to help protect sensitive electronic equipment from electrostatic discharge in technical environments.
- **High-seat / stool format**
  - Taller seat height suitable for sit-stand desks and elevated workstations.
- **Ergonomic design for long computer work**
  - Based on the Aeron chair’s ergonomic principles to support users over extended periods at a computer.
- **Application-specific use**
  - Intended for technical workstations, labs, or other environments where both ESD protection and ergonomic seating are needed.
- **Brand lineage**
  - Part of the Aeron family from Herman Miller, a line focused on task seating for work environments.
