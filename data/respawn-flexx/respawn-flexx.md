# RESPAWN FLEXX Gaming Chair

![RESPAWN FLEXX](https://www.huratips.com/images/respawn-flexx-1.png)

## Overview
The **RESPAWN FLEXX** is a high-back gaming chair meticulously crafted in collaboration with ergonomic experts and gaming professionals. It is designed to offer optimal comfort and support for extended gaming sessions.

## Features
- **Breathable Performance Mesh**: Ensures comfort and keeps you cool during intense gaming moments.
- **Adjustable Recline Resistance**: Allows for personalized recline settings to suit individual preferences.
- **Seat Depth Slider**: Enhances leg support by allowing for seat depth adjustments.
- **Ergonomic Design**: Includes a syncro tilt recline feature that promotes better spinal alignment and circulation.
- **Adjustable Head Support**: Features a height-adjustable and pivoting padded headrest for added comfort.
- **2D Adjustable Arms**: Adaptable armrests that can be adjusted in height and depth for a custom fit.
