## Features
- **Ergonomic design** aimed at improving comfort during extended computer use.  
- **Integrated lumbar support** to support the lower back.  
- **Retractable footrest** for leg support when reclining or resting.  
- **Modern visual design** intended to complement contemporary home and office setups.  
- **Designed for straightforward assembly**, with parts and construction oriented toward quick setup.  
- **Foam seat cushion** for padded sitting comfort (also noted to attract and retain pet hair).

## Known Issues & Limitations
- **Perceived structural flimsiness** during use.  
- **Flexible backrest** that noticeably bends or flexes under normal sitting/reclining.  
- **Loose lumbar support mechanism**, which can move around more than expected and may produce noise.  
- **Footrest rigidity concerns**, with the footrest reported to bend under the weight of the user.  
- **Seat material attracts pet hair**, with foam and fabric tending to collect significant pet hair over time.
