### Flexispot C7 (Premium Ergonomic Office Chair C7)

**Category:** Ergonomic office chairs  
**Brand:** Flexispot

#### Overview
The Flexispot C7 is an ergonomic computer chair designed for home and office use. It features lumbar support and an integrated footrest, with a modern visual design and relatively easy assembly. However, user reports indicate that the chair can feel structurally flimsy in everyday use.

#### Features
- **Ergonomic design** for computer and desk work
- **Intended use:** home office and traditional office environments
- **Modern aesthetic** styling
- **Lumbar support:** included, but reported to be loosely connected and prone to noise
- **Integrated footrest:** designed for leg support and reclining, but reported to bend noticeably under weight
- **Foam seat cushion:** provides padding but tends to collect significant pet hair
- **Backrest:** offers support but reportedly flexes noticeably during use
- **Assembly:** designed to be straightforward and easy to put together

#### Noted Drawbacks
- Backrest may feel unstable due to noticeable flex
- Lumbar support connection can be loose and noisy
- Footrest may lack rigidity and can bend under load
- Seat material/foam surface attracts and holds pet hair
