# Fractal Design Refine

## Overview
The **Fractal Design Refine** is a gaming chair crafted for comfort and durability, featuring a sleek and modern design. It is ideal for both gaming enthusiasts and professionals seeking ergonomic support during long hours of sitting.

## Features
- **Ergonomic Design:** Provides high-back support and a lumbar cushion for maximum comfort.
- **4D Adjustable Armrests:** Customize the armrests in four directions for a personalized fit.
- **Breathable Mesh Material:** Ensures optimal airflow, keeping you cool during intense gaming sessions.
- **Easy Assembly:** Designed for straightforward assembly with minimal tools required.
