# Anthros Chair V2

**Category:** Chair & Accessories  
**Brand:** Anthros  
**Website:** https://anthros.com

## Overview
The Anthros Chair V2 is a high-end ergonomic computer chair designed for people who sit for long periods, such as intensive computer users. It emphasizes spinal support, adjustable back segments, and long-duration seating comfort.

## Features
- Ergonomic design for extended computer use and long sitting sessions
- Independently adjustable upper and lower back supports
  - Supports a more natural lumbar curve
  - Allows separate tuning for lower and upper back comfort
- Thick Cloudfloat foam seat
  - Firm support with a plush, cushioned feel
- Optional 4D armrests (height, depth, width, and angle adjustments)
- One-on-one ergonomics video consultation included after purchase (to help optimize chair setup)

## Specifications
- Weight capacity: up to 300 lb (approx. 136 kg)
- Seat height range: 17.1" – 20.7" (approx. 43.4 – 52.6 cm)
