## Anthros Chair V2

**Category:** Chair accessories / ergonomic computer chair

### Overview
The Anthros Chair V2 is a high-end ergonomic computer chair designed for people who sit for extended periods, such as serious computer users and professionals. It focuses on back support, posture, and long-duration sitting comfort.

### Features
- Independently adjustable upper and lower back supports
- Backrest design intended to promote a natural lumbar curve
- Thick CloudFloat® foam seat for firm yet cushioned support
- Seat designed for long-duration sitting comfort
- Optional 4D armrests (height, depth, width, and pivot adjustments)
- One-on-one ergonomics video consultation included after purchase

### Specifications
- Weight capacity: up to 300 lb (approx. 136 kg)
- Seat height range: 17.1–20.7 in (43.4–52.6 cm)
- Warranty: 12 years
