## Anthros Chair V2

**Category:** Chair accessories / ergonomic office & gaming chair  
**Brand:** Anthros  
**Website:** [https://anthros.com](https://anthros.com)

### Overview
The Anthros Chair V2 is an ergonomic computer chair designed for people who sit for extended periods at a desk or gaming setup. It emphasizes adjustable spinal support, pressure‑reducing seat design, and durable construction intended for long‑duration comfort.

### Features
- **Independent dual backrest system**  
  - Separately adjustable upper and lower back supports.  
  - Aims to promote and maintain a natural lumbar curve.

- **Seat comfort & pressure management**  
  - Thick **Cloudfloat** foam cushion for firm yet plush seating.  
  - Seat design intended to reduce pressure points during long sitting sessions.  
  - Marketed as a low‑pressure cushion design relative to typical chair foam.

- **Ergonomic posture support**  
  - "Precision posture" system focused on aligning the spine while seated.  
  - Designed to support neutral posture over long durations.  
  - Includes a mode/adjustment approach described as "decompression" to change loading on the spine while seated.

- **Armrests (optional)**  
  - Optional 4D armrests (height, width, depth, and pivot adjustments) for forearm and shoulder support.

- **Materials & finish**  
  - Uses high‑performance upholstery fabrics intended for durability and comfort.

- **Regulatory status**  
  - Described by the manufacturer as an FDA‑registered chair.

- **Use cases**  
  - Designed for home or office computer work.  
  - Also promoted for gaming setups requiring extended sitting.

- **Post‑purchase ergonomics support**  
  - Includes a one‑on‑one ergonomics video consultation after purchase to help optimize chair setup and sitting posture.

### Specifications
- **Seat height range:** 17.1" – 20.7" (approx.)  
- **Weight capacity:** Up to 300 lb (approx. 136 kg)  
- **Armrests:** Optional 4D adjustable armrests  
- **Warranty:** 12‑year limited warranty (manufacturer‑stated)
