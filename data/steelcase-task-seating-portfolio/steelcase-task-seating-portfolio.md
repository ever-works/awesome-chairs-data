## Overview
The Steelcase Task Seating Portfolio is a family of high‑performance computer and task chairs designed for all‑day office work. The portfolio includes several Steelcase and AMQ task chairs, with a particular emphasis on comfort for users who sit for extended periods and on reducing embodied carbon through increased use of recycled plastics. The top seven Steelcase work chairs have been updated to approximately double their recycled content while maintaining ergonomic performance and build quality.

## Features
- **All‑day task seating:** Designed for users who spend most of the day seated at a computer or workstation.
- **Ergonomic support:** Portfolio focuses on task chairs that provide ergonomic support suitable for office and computer work (specific mechanisms vary by individual model).
- **High‑performance build quality:** Chairs are built for intensive, daily use in office environments.
- **Expanded recycled content:** The seven main Steelcase work chairs in the portfolio have been updated to approximately double their recycled content compared to previous versions.
- **Reduced embodied carbon:** Increased use of recycled plastics is intended to lower the embodied carbon of the chairs.
- **Customization options:** Each main Steelcase chair (Gesture, Leap, Steelcase Series 2, Amia + Amia Air, Steelcase Series 1, Think, Steelcase Karman) includes an online configurator for customizing options such as finishes and features (details depend on each model).
- **Buy‑online availability:** Many models in the portfolio are available for direct online purchase.

## Sustainability
- Focus on reducing embodied carbon via extensive use of recycled plastics in key task chairs.
- Approximate doubling of recycled content across the top seven Steelcase work chairs.
