# Eureka Ergonomic Computer & Gaming Chairs

**Brand:** Eureka Ergonomic  
**Category:** Office & Gaming Chairs  
**Website:** https://eurekaergonomic.com

## Overview
A range of ergonomic office and gaming chairs designed for people who spend long periods at a computer. These chairs are intended for both professional workstations and gaming setups, with a focus on long-duration comfort and posture support.

## Features
- **Ergonomic design:** Built specifically for extended computer use and all-day desk work.  
- **Lumbar support:** Emphasis on supporting the lower back to encourage healthier posture during long sessions.  
- **Adjustability:** Designed to allow users to fine-tune seating position (e.g., back support and overall sitting posture) to suit different body types and desk setups.  
- **Comfort for long sessions:** Intended to remain comfortable over many hours of continuous use, for both work and gaming.  
- **Suitable for mixed use:** Appropriate for home offices, professional workstations, and gaming environments.

## Typical Use Cases
- Daily office work at a computer.  
- Gaming sessions lasting several hours.  
- Home office or hybrid work environments where users remain seated most of the day.
