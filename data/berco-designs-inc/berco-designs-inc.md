# Berco Designs Inc.

## Overview
Berco Designs Inc. is a contract furniture manufacturer specializing in commercial tables, seating, booths, and related furnishings for offices, training rooms, and collaborative spaces where people spend extended periods at computers.

## Products & Services
- **Contract furniture manufacturing** for commercial interiors
- **Tables** for offices, training rooms, and collaborative spaces
- **Complementary seating** designed for extended computer and task use
- **Booths and office pods**, including:
  - Range Booth
  - Liberty Booth
- **Century Collection**:
  - Seating
  - Tables
  - Storage elements
  - Planters
- **Lounge seating** for informal or guest areas
- **Stools** for counters, bars, and high-top tables
- **Space division solutions** (e.g., partitions, booth configurations, pods)
- **Global chair sourcing program** for larger projects and consolidated chair procurement

## Features
- Focus on **commercial-grade durability** for contract environments
- Furniture suited to **offices, training rooms, and collaborative workspaces**
- Products designed for **extended computer and seated work**
- **Booth and pod solutions** that provide a degree of privacy while supporting comfort and visual cohesion
- **Integrated collections** (such as Century) combining seating, tables, storage, and planters for coordinated environments
- **Manufactured in St. Louis, Missouri**, with in-house control over production quality
- Backed by **over 50 years of engineering expertise** in contract furniture
- Emphasis on **high customization and personalization**, including tailored sizes, finishes, and configurations
- Support for **large-scale projects** via a dedicated global chair sourcing program

## Typical Use Cases
- Corporate offices and workstations
- Training and education rooms
- Collaborative and project spaces
- Lounge and guest seating areas
- Flexible, multi-use environments requiring coordinated furniture collections
