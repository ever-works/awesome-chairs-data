## Overview
The Herman Miller Setu Side Chair is a lightweight, flexible office and side chair designed to provide continuous support for computer and desk work. It uses a kinematic spine that responds automatically to changes in posture without requiring manual adjustments or complex mechanical controls.

---

## Features
- **Lightweight construction** – easier to move, reposition, and reconfigure in different spaces.  
- **Flexible design** – intended to accommodate a range of postures during computer or desk-based tasks.  
- **Kinematic spine** – engineered to flex and adjust in response to body movement.  
- **Automatic support adjustment** – chair adapts to posture changes without user-operated levers or dials.  
- **Minimal mechanical complexity** – designed to provide ergonomic support without complex mechanisms.
