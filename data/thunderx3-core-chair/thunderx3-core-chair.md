## Overview
The **ThunderX3 Core Chair** is meticulously designed for individuals who spend prolonged periods seated, ensuring superior ergonomic support and comfort. Its contemporary design and adaptability make it a versatile choice for both office and gaming environments.

- **Synchronous Mechanism**: Offers automatic back support, aligning with the user's posture.

- **Weight and Height Compatibility**: Suitable for users weighing up to 125kg and within the height range of 165-190cm.
