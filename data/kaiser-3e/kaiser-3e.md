### Description
The Kaiser 3E is an ergonomic chair designed for both office and gaming use. It emphasizes comfort and support for extended periods of sitting, providing an accessible option with features akin to the popular Kaiser 3 model but at a more budget-friendly price.

### Features
- **5cm Raised Lumbar Support:** Offers enhanced lower back support to reduce strain during long sitting sessions.
- **4D Armrests:** Adjustable armrests that move in four directions for personalized comfort.
- **Aluminum & Iron Wheelbases:** Durable and stable base construction for long-term use.
- **Cold-Cure Foam Seat:** Provides firm and consistent support, maintaining its shape over time.
- **Wide and Deep Seat:** Accommodates various body types, ensuring comfort for extended periods.
