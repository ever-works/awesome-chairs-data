## RESPAWN 3085 Gaming Chair

![RESPAWN 3085](https://cdn.shopify.com/s/files/1/2589/6928/products/respawn-3085-1.jpg)

### Overview
The **RESPAWN 3085** is designed as an entry-level gaming chair that combines comfort with style. It's perfect for both intense gaming sessions and long workdays, offering features that ensure support and relaxation.

### Features
- **Design & Material**: Upholstered in bonded leather with colored mesh accents and black stitching for a sleek, aggressive look.
- **Padding**: Contoured segmented padding that conforms to your body, providing quality support.
- **Armrests**: Flip-up padded armrests for convenience and added comfort.
- **Tilt & Recline**: Offers a 15-degree tilt recline for relaxation.
- **Adjustability**:
  - Seat height adjustment.
  - 360-degree swivel capability.
  - Integrated padded headrest.
- **Weight Capacity**: Supports up to 275 lbs.

### Dimensions
- **Height**: Adjustable from 44.5" to 48.25"
- **Width**: 28.25"
- **Depth**: 30.5"
- **Seat Height**: Adjustable from 17" to 20.75"
- **Seat Size**: 20" W x 18.75" D
- **Back Size**: 19.6" x 29.3"
