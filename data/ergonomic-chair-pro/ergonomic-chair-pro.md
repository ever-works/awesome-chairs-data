### Ergonomic Chair Pro

**Brand:** Branch  
**Category:** Chair Accessories  
**Source:** https://www.branchfurniture.com/products/ergonomic-chair-pro

#### Description
The Ergonomic Chair Pro is a mid-priced, highly adjustable office chair designed for people who sit for extended periods. It combines a high-density foam seat with a contoured mesh back and multiple adjustment points to support a wide range of body types and working styles.

#### Features
- **5D adjustable armrests**:
  - Height adjustment
  - Depth (forward/back) adjustment
  - Width (in/out) adjustment
  - Angle (pivot) adjustment
  - Removable armrests
- **Adjustable seat depth** to accommodate different body heights
- **Lockable recline** for stable leaning positions
- **Seat tilt** for more active sitting and posture changes
- **Tunable lumbar support** for lower-back comfort
- **High-density foam seat** for cushioning and support
- **Contoured mesh back** for support and breathability
- **Multiple upholstery options**:
  - Flocked Italian double mesh
  - Vegan leather
  - Real leather
- **Powder-coated aluminum base** for durability
- **Smooth casters** for easy rolling
- **Optional tall cylinder** to extend height range
- **Optional headrest** for added neck and head support
- **7-year warranty**
