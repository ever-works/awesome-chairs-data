# PL4800 Ergonomic Gaming Chair

![PL4800](https://www.vertagear.com/images/pl4800.jpg)

## Overview
The **PL4800** is a high-quality ergonomic gaming chair designed by **Vertagear**. Part of the renowned 800 Series, this chair is crafted to offer durability and comfort, especially for larger individuals.

## Features
- **Ergonomic Design**: Tailored to provide optimal comfort and support for prolonged gaming sessions.
- **Robust Build**: Capable of supporting users up to 360 lbs.
- **Height Accommodation**: Suitable for individuals between 5'9" to 6'6".
- **Durable Materials**: Constructed with high-quality materials ensuring longevity and resilience.
