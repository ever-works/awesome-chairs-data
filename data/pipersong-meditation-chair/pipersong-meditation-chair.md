### Pipersong Meditation Chair

**Category:** Others  
**Brand:** pipersong

**Description**  
An ergonomic, non-traditional computer chair designed for people who sit for long periods in alternative positions. It supports cross-legged, kneeling, squatting, one-leg-up, and other unconventional postures while working at a desk, and can also function as a regular office chair.

#### Features
- Designed specifically for multiple sitting positions, including:
  - Cross-legged
  - Squatting
  - Kneeling
  - One leg up / asymmetrical postures
- 360-degree swiveling footstool to support varied leg positions
- Allows frequent posture changes throughout the day
- Provides an active, flexible seating setup that encourages movement while seated
- Supports unconventional sitting habits for users who find standard office or gaming chairs uncomfortable
- Can be converted into a regular chair by tucking the footstool behind and placing feet flat on the floor
- Suitable for long-hour desk work where varied posture is desired
