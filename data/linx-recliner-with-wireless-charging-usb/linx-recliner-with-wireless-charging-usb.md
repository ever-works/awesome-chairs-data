## Overview
The Linx is a power glider recliner designed as a tech-focused lounge and gaming chair. It integrates device charging directly into the armrest area and uses Techno 3D fabric for a more comfortable, durable seating surface during long gaming or computer sessions. An optional phone mount is available to further enhance hands‑free use.

---

## Features
- **Power glider recliner**
  - Motorized reclining mechanism (power recline)
  - Gliding motion for added comfort
- **Integrated charging**
  - Built‑in wireless charging pad for compatible phones/devices
  - USB ports for wired device charging
- **Techno 3D fabric**
  - Modern fabric designed for comfort and breathability
  - Suited for extended sitting periods (gaming, streaming, computer work)
- **Optional phone mount**
  - Mounting solution for phone placement and viewing (optional accessory)
- **Use case focus**
  - Designed specifically for tech‑heavy setups
  - Intended for long gaming or computer sessions
