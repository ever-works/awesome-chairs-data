# EWin Racing

**Website:** https://www.ewinracing.com

EWin Racing designs and manufactures ergonomic, racing-style gaming and office chairs built for prolonged computer use. Their chairs focus on heavy-duty construction, long-term durability, and comfort for both typical and plus-sized users.

---

## Features

### Ergonomic Design
- Racing-style gaming and office chairs
- High backrests for full upper-body support
- Thick cushioning for extended sitting sessions
- Designed for prolonged computer use (gaming and office work)
- Focus on comfort for hardcore gamers and plus-sized users

### Heavy-Duty Construction
- Engineered for durability and long service life
- Chairs support up to approximately **550 lbs** (plus-sized friendly)
- Suitable for heavy-duty work and intensive gaming

### Adjustability & Comfort
- Adjustable armrests (for customized arm and shoulder support)
- High back with ergonomic support to maintain posture
- Designed to provide seamless support through a range of seated activities

### Materials
- Durable leather (or leather-like) upholstery around a foam core
- Stain-resistant surface
- Foam core provides structured support and breathability

### Performance & Testing (Internal Standards vs. ANSI/BIFMA X5.1)
EWin Racing reports internal testing standards that exceed common office-chair industry benchmarks:

- **Back Strength Test**  
  - EWin standard: Structural integrity stable under **1001 N (~355 lbs)** of pressure for 1 minute  
  - ANSI/BIFMA X5.1 standard: **225 lbs**

- **Seating Durability Test**  
  - EWin standard: Structural integrity largely intact when a **193 kg (425 lbs)** weight is dropped from 6 inches onto the center of the seat  
  - ANSI/BIFMA X5.1 standard: **300 lbs**

- **Class 4 Hydraulics Durability**  
  - EWin standard: Full serviceability after **120,000 full 360° rotations** at 5–15 RPM with a **181.5 kg (400 lbs)** load, seat at lowest height  
  - ANSI/BIFMA X5.1 standard load reference: **270 lbs**

- **Aluminum Base Strength**  
  - EWin standard: Structural integrity preserved under **11,120 N (~2,500 lbs)** of pressure for 1 minute, weight removed and re-applied for another minute without structural compromise

### Use Cases
- PC and console gaming setups
- Home office and professional office workstations
- Users requiring high weight capacity and robust build
- Individuals seeking a long-lasting, heavy-duty chair solution
