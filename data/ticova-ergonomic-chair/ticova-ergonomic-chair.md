# Ticova Ergonomic Chair

![Ticova Ergonomic Chair](https://example.com/ticova-image1.jpg)

## Overview
The **Ticova Ergonomic Chair** is an affordable option for individuals seeking a comfortable and adjustable seating solution for their workspace. With a focus on ergonomic support, this chair is designed to enhance comfort and posture during long hours of sitting.

## Features
- **Adjustable Headrest:** Customize the head support for optimal comfort.
- **Versatile Adjustments:** Offers multiple adjustment options to tailor the chair to individual needs.
- **Ergonomic Design:** Includes features like lumbar support to alleviate back pain and promote a healthy posture.
- **High-Density Shaping Foam:** A thick 3-inch foam seat provides comfort for extended periods of sitting.
- **Breathable Mesh Back:** Allows for air circulation, keeping the user cool.
- **Flexible Arms:** Adjustable armrests that can be moved according to preference.

## Pricing
- **Budget-Friendly:** Priced at approximately **$199** on Amazon, providing value under the $200 mark.
