## ICON Series Gaming Chair by Noblechairs

![ICON Series Gaming Chair](https://www.noblechairs.com/images/icon-series-gaming-chair-1.jpg)

### Description
The ICON Series Gaming Chair by noblechairs showcases a distinctive Nuka-Cola Quantum Edition design. This chair is crafted with vegan PU leather and features a robust construction that can support up to 150 kg. It offers an immersive gaming experience with its high-quality materials and beloved Fallout series aesthetic.

### Features
- **Design:** Nuka-Cola Quantum Edition, inspired by the Fallout series.
- **Material:** Made with vegan PU leather for a premium feel and durability.
- **Adjustability:** Equipped with 4D armrests to cater to various seating preferences.
- **Mobility:** Features XL casters for smooth movement on multiple surfaces.
- **Weight Capacity:** Supports up to 150 kg, accommodating a wide range of users.
