## Overview
The **AutoFull C3** is a gaming chair crafted for those who prioritize back comfort and seek a snug fit. Designed with gamers in mind, especially those with wider shoulders, this chair ensures comfort for extended gaming sessions.

[View Product](https://autofull.com/products/c3)

## Features
- **Back Comfort:** Engineered to provide excellent support for the back, enhancing comfort during long hours of use.
- **Snug Fit:** Equipped with bolsters that offer a secure yet comfortable fit, ideal for users with wider shoulders.
- **Extended Use Comfort:** Maintains comfort for up to 6 hours, making it suitable for lengthy gaming sessions.
- **Temperature Considerations:** While initially comfortable, users may notice the chair feeling warmer over extended periods.
