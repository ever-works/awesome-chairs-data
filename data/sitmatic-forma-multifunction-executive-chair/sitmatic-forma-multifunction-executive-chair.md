# Sitmatic Forma Multifunction Executive Chair

![Sitmatic Logo](/path/to/sitmatic/logo.png)

## Overview
The **Sitmatic Forma Multifunction Executive Chair** is a highly adaptable office chair designed for ergonomic support and comfort during extended use. It is available in both medium and standard seat sizes, making it versatile for various user preferences.

[View Product](https://www.officechairsusa.com/sitmatic-forma-multifunction-executive-with-medium-or-standard-seat/)

## Features
- **Ergonomic Design:** Provides support for long hours of sitting.
- **Multi-Function Mechanism:**
  - Center tilt rocking mode.
  - Backrest angle adjustable from 89° to 105°.
  - Seat angle adjusts from -5° to 2°.
  - Tension adjustable with locking capability in 6 positions.
  - Backrest height adjustable in 27 positions.
  - Seat depth adjustable in 6 positions.
  - Pneumatic seat height adjustment.
- **Arm Options:**
  - **+1A:** Adjustable arm height (4") and width (2" per side) with 360° pivot and contoured caps.
  - **+6K:** Foldable arms with adjustable height (3") and soft gel caps.
- **Additional Options:**
  - Inflatable lumbar bolster.
  - Tailbone seat cutout.
  - Memory foam seat topper.
  - Upholstered headrest.

## Brand
**Sitmatic** is renowned for its ergonomically designed furniture, originally rooted in Italian craftsmanship, now manufactured in California. Their chairs are constructed with steel frames and components, supporting users of any weight, and come with a lifetime component warranty.
