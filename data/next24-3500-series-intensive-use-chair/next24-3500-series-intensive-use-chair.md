# NEXT24 3500 Series – Intensive Use Chair

## Description
A heavy-duty ergonomic task chair designed for 24/7 intensive-use environments such as control rooms and offices where users sit for extended periods. The NEXT24 3500 Series uses BodyBilt’s contoured seat and back with SKYDEX technology to help reduce pressure, pain, and fatigue during prolonged computer work.

## Features
- **Intensive-use design** for 24/7 applications and mission-critical workstations.
- **Executive back** with BodyBilt’s pressure-reducing contoured backrest.
- **SKYDEX foam technology**:
  - Uses engineered geometrics to distribute seated pressure.
  - More breathable to help reduce moisture buildup while sitting.
  - Rated for a lifecycle up to 10× longer than standard foam (per manufacturer).
- **Seat options (4 sizes)**:
  - Moderately Contoured Standard Seat (#7 seat).
  - Standard Flat Seat (#8 seat).
  - Big and Tall – XL Wide Seat (#4 seat).
  - Big and Tall – XL Tall “Stretch” Seat (#9 seat).
- **Lumbar support**:
  - Built-in Air Lumbar system.
  - Lumbar height adjustment range: 3.5".
- **Backrest adjustability**:
  - Backrest height: 20.00".
  - Backrest height adjustment range: 4.00".
  - Backrest angle adjustment: 12°.
  - Lumbar region width/height area: 20.00".
  - Shoulder region width: 19.00".
- **Seat adjustability**:
  - Seat depth options: 21.00" or 23.00" (depending on configuration).
  - Seat depth adjustment range: 2.50".
  - Seat height adjustment range: 3.50".
  - Seat height range: 17.00" to 20.50".
- **Mechanism**:
  - Intensive-Use (I) mechanism designed for heavy-duty, continuous operation.
- **Arms**:
  - Optima 24/7 arms with heavy-duty S'port pad (ASPTL/).
- **Base & mobility**:
  - Marquis base, black, 27" diameter (B2).
  - 360° swivel.
  - Chrome casters, 2" (C7).
- **Cylinders & capacity**:
  - Heavy-duty short-height cylinder (YHDS).
  - Configurations available with 300 lb and 500 lb weight capacities.
- **Foam**:
  - SKYDEX cushioning (SKYDEX) throughout seating surface.
- **Fabrics (quick-ship options)**:
  - Grade 01 Staccato – Navy.
  - Grade 01 Staccato – Black.
  - Grade 01 Staccato – Sandy.
  - Grade 04 Brisa – Black Onyx.
  - Grade 04 Brisa – Night Navy.
- **Quick-ship availability**:
  - Standard model BodyBilt chairs can be built and shipped within five working days (limited quantities and fabric selections, per manufacturer).
