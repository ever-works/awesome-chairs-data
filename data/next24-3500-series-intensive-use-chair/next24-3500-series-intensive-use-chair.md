#### Description
A heavy-duty ergonomic task chair designed for 24/7 intensive-use environments such as control rooms, healthcare, dispatch, and offices where users remain seated for long periods. The NEXT24 3500 Series combines BodyBilt’s pressure-reducing contoured seat and back with SKYDEX technology to improve pressure distribution, durability, and comfort during extended computer or monitoring work.

---

#### Features
- **24/7 intensive-use design** for mission-critical and continuous-operation environments.  
- **BodyBilt contoured seat and back** to reduce pressure, pain, and fatigue during long sitting sessions.  
- **SKYDEX technology in foam**:
  - Uses engineered geometries to distribute seated pressure more evenly.  
  - Enhances breathability to reduce moisture accumulation while sitting.  
  - Rated at up to 10× the lifecycle of standard foam (increased durability for intensive use).  
- **Executive back style** with ergonomic shaping for lumbar and shoulder support.  
- **Adjustable air lumbar support** (Air Lumbar) for customizable lower back support.  
- **Intensive-use mechanism** (I mechanism) for heavy-duty, continuous adjustments and performance.  
- **Four seat size/shape options** to fit different body types and preferences:
  - Moderately Contoured Standard Seat (#7 seat).  
  - Standard Flat Seat (#8 seat).  
  - Big and Tall – XL Wide Seat (#4 seat).  
  - Big and Tall – XL Tall/Stretch Seat (#9 seat).  
- **Optima 24/7 arms** with heavy-duty S’port pads for arm and forearm support under continuous use.  
- **Heavy-duty cylinder – short height** for enhanced strength and stability.  
- **Marquis 27" black base** (B2) for a wide, stable footprint.  
- **Chrome 2" casters** suitable for smooth rolling and frequent movement.  
- **360° swivel** for full rotation and task reach without over-twisting the body.  
- **Weight-capacity options:** configurations rated up to **300 lb** or **500 lb** depending on model selection.  
- **Five-day Quick Ship** available for standard models in select fabrics (limited quantities and fabric options).  
- **Backed by BodyBilt’s Limited Life Warranty** (coverage details from manufacturer).
