# AndaSeat x NRG Gaming Chair

![AndaSeat Logo](https://www.andaseat.com/logo.png)

## Overview
The **AndaSeat x NRG Gaming Chair** is a collaborative product designed in partnership with NRG Esports. This chair is specifically engineered for competitive gamers, blending high-performance design with ergonomic features.

![AndaSeat x NRG Gaming Chair](https://www.andaseat.com/images/nrg-chair-1.png)

## Features
- **Pop-out Lumbar Support**: Offers an adjustable lumbar support system with an any-angle lock for personalized comfort.
- **6D Armrests**: These armrests are designed for maximum adjustability, allowing users to customize their gaming posture.
- **Stain-resistant Sustainable Leather**: The chair is upholstered with eco-friendly leather that resists stains, ensuring durability and easy maintenance.
