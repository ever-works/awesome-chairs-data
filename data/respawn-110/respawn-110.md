## RESPAWN 110 Gaming Chair

![RESPAWN 110](https://www.huratips.com/images/respawn-110-pro-1.png)

### Overview
The **RESPAWN 110 Pro** is a racing-style gaming chair designed for prolonged comfort and style. It is ideal for gamers who spend long hours at their desks, combining ergonomic features with a sleek design.

### Features
- **Reclining Back:** Offers a 135-degree recline with infinite angle lock for customized comfort.
- **Extendable Footrest:** Provides additional support for your legs during extended gaming sessions.
- **Adjustable Headrest & Lumbar Support:** Ensures personalized comfort and ergonomic support.
- **Integrated Lumbar Support:** Contoured endurance foam seat for secure and comfortable seating.
- **Durable Fabric:** Available in low-maintenance, durable fabric options for a modern look.
- **Weight Capacity:** Supports up to 275 lbs, ensuring stability and durability.
- **Dimensions:**  
  - Height: 50 - 53.7"
  - Width: 28.7"
  - Depth: 26.4"
  - Arm Height: 26 - 29.9"
  - Seat Height: 18.5 - 22.4"
  - Seat Size: 21.5" W x 22.2" D
  - Back Size: 30.9" x 32.7"
