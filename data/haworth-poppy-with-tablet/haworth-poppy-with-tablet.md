# Haworth Poppy with Tablet

## Overview
Haworth Poppy with Tablet is a casual lounge/guest chair designed for relaxed but productive use in collaborative and touchdown spaces. It combines softer, residential-inspired styling with enough ergonomic support and integrated surface space for light to moderate computer or tablet work.

## Features
- **Integrated tablet arm** for convenient use of laptops, tablets, or notebooks
- **Casual lounge/guest chair design** suitable for informal work and meeting areas
- **Residential-style aesthetic** to create a softer, less formal environment
- **Supportive seating** intended for light to moderate-duration computer or tablet work sessions
- **Optimized for collaborative and touchdown spaces**, such as informal meeting zones, lobbies, and focus nooks

## Recommended Uses
- Collaborative work areas
- Touchdown and short-stay workstations
- Informal meeting spaces
- Lounge, guest, or waiting areas where occasional focused work is needed
