## Staples Hyken

### Overview
The Staples Hyken is a budget-friendly ergonomic office chair with a breathable mesh back and seat, designed for long hours at a desk in home or office settings. It includes lumbar support and a headrest, and offers reclining functionality for added comfort.

### Features
- Ergonomic swivel task chair design
- Breathable mesh back and seat for improved airflow
- Reclining function for adjustable seating position
- Adjustable office chair (various settings for personalized fit)
- Integrated lumbar support
- Included headrest for neck support
- Suitable for both home and office use
- Designed for long-duration computer/desk work
- Mesh material may compress over time with heavy use

- Frequently discounted to around **$140 USD** (actual price may vary over time and by retailer)
