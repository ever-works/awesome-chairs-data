## Staples Hyken

**Category:** Chair Accessories  
**Brand:** Staples  
**Type:** Ergonomic mesh office/task chair

### Overview
The Staples Hyken is a budget-friendly ergonomic office chair with a breathable mesh back and seat, designed for comfortable use over long work sessions at a desk in either home or office settings.

### Features
- Ergonomic swivel task chair design
- Breathable mesh back
- Breathable mesh seat
- Reclining function
- Adjustable office chair (multiple settings for personal comfort)
- Integrated headrest
- Built-in lumbar support
- Suitable for home or office workstations
