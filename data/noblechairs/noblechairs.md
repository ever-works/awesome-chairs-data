# Noblechairs

## Overview
Noblechairs designs premium gaming and office chairs for people who spend long hours at a desk or computer. The brand focuses on ergonomic design, high-quality materials, and long-term seating comfort, drawing on design principles used in high-class car seat manufacturing.

## Features
- **Product Types & Series**
  - LEGEND Series gaming chairs
  - HERO Series gaming chairs
  - HERO ST Series
  - EPIC Series gaming chairs
  - EPIC Compact Series (smaller form factor)
  - ICON Series
  - Special and licensed editions (e.g., DOOM The Dark Ages, Starfield, e-sports editions)
  - Accessories such as pillow sets

- **Design & Ergonomics**
  - Ergonomic design intended for extended desk and computer use
  - Inspired by high-class car seat manufacturing
  - Focus on posture support and long-term sitting comfort

- **Materials & Build**
  - Use of high-quality, durable materials across chair ranges
  - Emphasis on excellent build quality and refined finish

- **Aesthetic & Variants**
  - Classy, refined visual design
  - Multiple editions and color variants per series (e.g., DAWN TX Granite, HERO Black Edition, EPIC TX)
  - Themed and licensed designs for specific franchises and e-sports teams
