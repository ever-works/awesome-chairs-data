# Sitmatic GoodFit Mesh Back Chair

![Sitmatic Logo](/path/to/sitmatic-logo.png)

### Overview
The Sitmatic GoodFit Mesh Back Chair is an ergonomic office chair designed to provide personalized comfort and support. With a focus on adaptability, it suits a wide range of users, promoting a comfortable and healthy seating posture for extended periods.

### Features
- **Ergonomic Design**: Tailored to adapt to various body types, accommodating users from 5'3" to 6'5" and beyond.
- **Mesh Back**: Enhances airflow, keeping you cool while seated.
- **Standard Seat**: Ensures comfort during long hours of use.
- **Heavy-Duty Construction**: Built with steel frames and components, supporting any user without weight limitations.
- **Customizable Options**:
  - Inflatable lumbar bolster for additional back support.
  - Tailbone seat cutout for pressure relief.
  - Memory foam seat topper for enhanced cushioning.
  - Optional seat slider with OT mechanism for personalized seat depth.
- **Arm Options**:
  - **+7K**: Adjustable arm height (4") and width (2" per side) with soft gel caps.
  - **+6K**: Foldable arms with adjustable height (3") and soft gel caps.
- **Warranty**: Lifetime warranty on components, ensuring durability and longevity.

### Brand
