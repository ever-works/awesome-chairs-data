## Overview

The Herman Miller Aeron Gaming Chair is a gaming-focused configuration of the classic Aeron chair. It’s designed for long gaming and computer sessions, combining Aeron’s well-known ergonomic engineering with adjustments and styling aimed at gamers who sit for extended periods.

- **Encourages natural movement**, supporting posture changes instead of a fixed sitting position.
