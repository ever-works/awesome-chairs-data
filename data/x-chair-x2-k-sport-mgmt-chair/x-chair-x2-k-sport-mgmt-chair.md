## X-Chair X2 K-Sport Mgmt Chair

![X-Chair Logo](https://www.xchair.com/logo.png)

### Description
The **X-Chair X2 K-Sport Mgmt Chair** is meticulously designed for individuals who spend extensive hours at their desks. This ergonomic chair is tailored to enhance comfort and productivity with its advanced features.

### Features
- **Dynamic Variable Lumbar Support**: This feature ensures your lower back is consistently supported, adapting to your movements for continuous comfort.
- **SciFloat Infinite Recline**: Experience weightless reclining that promotes natural movement, reducing fatigue over prolonged sitting periods.
- **ELEMAX Technology Compatibility**: Upgrade your chair with cooling, heat, and massage features for an unmatched sitting experience.
- **X-HMT Technology Compatibility**: Enhance your chair with therapeutic massage and heat for additional relaxation and wellness benefits.
- **Breathable Mesh Material**: Designed to ventilate heat effectively, keeping you cool during long hours of sitting.
- **Adjustable Headrest**: Provides pivoting support, allowing you to position it for optimal neck and head comfort.
- **Pivot Spring Lumbar Support**: Adjusts dynamically to your movements, ensuring continuous back support.
