# BodyBilt Ergonomic Office Seating

Premium ergonomic office chairs designed for intensive computer and office work, with contoured, pressure-reducing seats and extensive adjustability to fit a wide range of body types and workstation layouts.

**Brand:** BodyBilt  
**Category:** Chair Accessories / Ergonomic Office Seating  
**Source:** https://bodybilt.com/ergonomic-seating/by-application/office/

## Overview
BodyBilt Ergonomic Office Seating is a family of office chairs and workstools built for people who sit at computers for long periods. The line includes executive chairs, task chairs, mesh-back chairs, workstools, and petite-back models, allowing users to match chair type and configuration to their body size and work style.

## Applications
- General office and computer workstations
- Executive and managerial offices
- Task-focused and knowledge work
- Users who sit for prolonged periods
- Users needing petite back options
- Higher-height applications (e.g., drafting / workstool configurations)

## Features
- **Ergonomic design for long-duration sitting**
  - Intended for users who sit at computers and office desks for much of the day.
- **Contoured, pressure-reducing seats**
  - Seat shapes designed to distribute pressure more evenly and reduce pressure points.
- **Advanced adjustability**
  - Multiple adjustment points to fine-tune fit and posture (e.g., seat and back adjustments; specifics vary by model).
- **Modular customization options**
  - Configurations can be tailored to individual body types and specific workstation needs.
- **Variety of chair types**
  - Executive back chairs
  - Manager back chairs
  - Task chairs
  - Mesh-back task and office chairs
  - Workstools for higher work surfaces
  - Petite-back executive and office chairs for smaller users
- **Back style options (model-dependent)**
  - High/"executive" backs
  - Manager/petite backs
  - Mesh back variants
- **Office-focused ergonomics**
  - All models are intended for office and computer use, rather than industrial or specialty environments.
