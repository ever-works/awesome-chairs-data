# BodyBilt Ergonomic Office Seating

## Summary
BodyBilt Ergonomic Office Seating is a line of premium office chairs designed for people who sit at computers for extended periods. The range emphasizes contoured, pressure-reducing seats, advanced adjustability, and modular customization to match different body types and workstation setups.

## Product Range / Models
BodyBilt offers multiple ergonomic office seating styles and series, including:

- **Classic 3500 Series – Executive Back Chair**  
- **Aircelli 2700 Series – Mesh Back Task Chair**  
- **Midcelli 2800 Series – Mesh Back Chair**  
- **Classic 2500 Series – Manager Back Chair**  
- **Midcelli 2800 Series – Mesh Back Workstool**  
- **Sola 2600 Series – Task Chair**  
- **Sola 3600 Series – Executive Task Chair**  
- **SOLA LT – Task Chair**  
- **Classic 3400 Series – Petite Back Executive Chair**  
- **Classic 2400 Series – Petite Back Chair**

These models include different back styles (executive, manager, petite, midback), surface types (mesh back, contoured seating), and formats (task chairs, executive chairs, workstools) for varied office roles and body sizes.

## Features
- **Designed for intensive computer and office work**  
  - Intended for users who sit at computers for much of the day.

- **Contoured, pressure-reducing seats**  
  - Seat designs aim to distribute pressure more evenly to improve comfort during prolonged sitting.

- **Advanced adjustability**  
  - Adjustability is designed to accommodate different body types and workstation configurations (e.g., varying desk setups and tasks).

- **Modular customization options**  
  - Components can be configured to fit individual ergonomic needs and preferences.

- **Multiple ergonomic back and chair formats**  
  - Executive back, manager back, petite back, and midback options.  
  - Mesh back options in the Aircelli and Midcelli series.  
  - Task chairs, executive task chairs, and workstools for different work styles and environments.

- **Ergonomic design goals** (as described for ergonomic office chairs on the source page):  
  - Help correct spinal alignment.  
  - Reduce slouching and head-forward posture.  
  - Support the trunk.  
  - Encourage proper pelvic alignment.  
  - Support the arms.  
  - Work with human anatomy to reduce physical stressors associated with sitting.

- **Intended health and comfort benefits**  
  - Provide better support to help users avoid pain and maintain comfort during work.  
  - Support productivity and overall well-being when seated for work (within the limits of proper ergonomic use and posture).

## Typical Applications
- General office seating.  
- Computer workstations.  
- Roles requiring prolonged but supported sitting (e.g., administrative, managerial, task-focused desk work).
