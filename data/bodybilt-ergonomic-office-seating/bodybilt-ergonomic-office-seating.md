# BodyBilt Ergonomic Office Seating

## Description
A line of premium ergonomic office chairs from BodyBilt designed for people who sit at computers for extended periods. The seating is intended for intensive computer and office work, emphasizing posture support, pressure reduction, and configurability for different body types and workstations.

## Features
- **Ergonomic design for office use** to support people who sit at computers for long periods.
- **Contoured, pressure-reducing seats** designed to distribute weight and reduce pressure points.
- **Advanced adjustability** to better fit individual body dimensions and workstation setups.
- **Modular customization options** to configure chairs for specific body types and tasks.
- **Posture support goals** typical of ergonomic office chairs:
  - Support for spinal alignment.
  - Reduction of slouching and head‑forward posture.
  - Support for the trunk.
  - Encouragement of proper pelvic alignment.
  - Support for the arms.
- **Variety of back styles and formats**, including:
  - Executive back chairs.
  - Manager back chairs.
  - Petite back chairs sized for smaller users.
  - Mesh back task chairs.
  - Mesh back mid-height chairs.
  - Mesh back workstools.
  - Standard task chairs and executive task chairs.
- **Designed for intensive computer and office work**, where users may remain seated for long portions of the day.
