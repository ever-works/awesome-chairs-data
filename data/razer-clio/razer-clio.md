# Razer Clio

**Category:** Others  
**Brand:** Razer  

## Overview
The Razer Clio is a THX Spatial Audio–enabled wireless speaker head cushion designed to mount on a chair headrest. It combines near-field speakers and plush cushioning to provide immersive, comfortable audio for gaming, movies, and music without wearing a headset.

## Features

### Integrated Near‑Field Dual Speakers
- Dual angled speakers positioned near the ears.  
- Provides headset-like immersion without wearing headphones.  
- Thick, plush padding around the speakers for added comfort.

### THX® Spatial Audio–Enabled
- Supports advanced THX immersive audio.  
- Acoustic widening modes for a larger, more realistic soundstage.  
- Designed to enhance positional audio and overall realism for games, movies, and music.

### 3 Distinct EQ Modes
- **Game Mode**  
  - Emphasizes teammate voice communications.  
  - Enhances key gameplay audio cues such as footsteps.
- **Movies Mode**  
  - Fuller soundstage for cinematic content and games.  
  - Enhanced deeper, richer bass for explosions and environmental effects.
- **Music Mode**  
  - Tuned for music playback.  
  - Dynamic bass response and clear vocals.

### Flexible Audio Setup
- Can function as a **primary speaker**.  
- Can be used as a **rear surround speaker** to complement an existing soundbar or 2.0/2.1 speaker system.  
- Adjustable to fit chair headrests with a circumference between **47 cm and 65 cm**.

### Dual 43 mm Full‑Range Drivers
- Two 43 mm full‑range drivers with aluminum cones.  
- Includes passive radiators for enhanced low-end response.  
- Tuned for clear trebles and deep, punchy bass.

### Wireless Connectivity
- **Razer HyperSpeed Wireless (2.4 GHz)** for low‑latency PC and console gaming.  
- **Bluetooth** for use with Android, iOS, and other Bluetooth‑enabled devices.  
- Source button to switch between available input modes.

### Battery & Charging
- Up to **14 hours of battery life** on a single full charge.  
- USB‑C charging support (USB‑C cable not included).

### On‑Device Controls
- **Volume control buttons** (+ / –).  
- **Power button**.  
- **EQ mode button** to cycle between Game, Movie, and Music modes.  
- **Source button** to switch audio inputs.
