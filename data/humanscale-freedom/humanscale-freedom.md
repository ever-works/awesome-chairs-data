## Humanscale Freedom Chair Summary

### Overview
The **Humanscale Freedom** chair is a premium ergonomic office chair renowned for its advanced design and comfort. It is crafted for professionals who prioritize health and comfort in their workspace, offering a blend of aesthetic appeal and functional ergonomics.

### Features
- **Design and Assembly**: Arrives nearly fully assembled, requiring minimal setup.
- **Ergonomic Adjustments**:
  - **Seat Adjustment**: Slide forward or backward using a lever under the seat.
  - **Height Adjustment**: Simple lever on the seat's right side.
  - **Backrest**: Adjustable on rails with a pivot to conform to the user's back.
  - **Headrest**: Height adjustable and automatically adjusts as you recline.
- **Armrests**:
  - Standard armrests with height adjustment.
  - Advanced armrests feature slide tracks for inward/outward and forward/backward movement.
- **Reclining System**: Utilizes a weight-based resistance system, adjusting automatically based on user weight.
- **Materials and Upholstery**: Available in various colors and fabrics, with options for cushioned or rubberized finishes.
