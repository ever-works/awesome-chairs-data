# HERO TX Anthracite

![HERO TX Anthracite](https://www.noblechairs.com/assets/images/hero-tx-anthracite.jpg)

The **HERO TX Anthracite** by **noblechairs** is a premium gaming and office chair designed for those seeking both comfort and style. Crafted with breathable fabric, this chair offers a range of features to enhance your sitting experience.

### Features

- **Breathable Fabric**: The TX Series features a specially developed breathable fabric that ensures high breathability, durability, and a softer feel.
- **Ergonomic Design**: Built-in adjustable lumbar support and an integrated memory foam headrest provide superior ergonomics.
- **4D Armrests**: Enlarged armrests that can be adjusted in four dimensions for optimal positioning.
- **Durable Construction**: Fleece backing enhances durability while maintaining comfort.
- **Stain Protection**: Easy-to-care-for fabric with built-in stain protection.
- **Weight Capacity**: Designed to support users up to 150 kg (330 lbs).
- **Included Accessories**: Comes with a head and lumbar pillow set for additional comfort.
