# Haworth Zody II Ergonomic Chair

![Haworth Logo](https://www.haworth.com/content/dam/haworth/na/en/images/logos/haworth-logo.png)

## Overview
The **Haworth Zody II** is an advanced ergonomic chair designed to offer users superior comfort and support. This chair is ideal for both seated and perched positions, making it suitable for dynamic office environments.

![Zody II](https://www.haworth.com/content/dam/haworth/na/en/images/products/seating/task/zody-ii/zody-ii-hero.jpg)

## Features
- **3-Point Synchronous Tilt System**: Aligns with the body's natural pivot points for smooth reclining.
- **Asymmetrical Lumbar Support**: Provides personalized comfort and encourages a natural sitting posture.
- **Pelvic Support System**: Ensures consistent lumbar support and balanced feel.
- **Adaptive Suspension Mesh**: Offers targeted support with three distinct ergonomic zones.
- **Torsional Flex Technology**: Allows dynamic backrest response to position shifts, reducing pressure points.
- **Adjustable Features**:
  - Seat Height
  - Seat Depth
  - 4D Arms
  - Back Stop
  - Tilt Tension
  - Forward Tilt
- **Optional Features**:
  - Patented Asymmetrical Lumbar Support
  - Dual Posture
