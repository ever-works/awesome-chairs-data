# ContourMax Lumbar

![ContourMax Logo](/images/contourmax-logo.png)

## Overview
The **ContourMax Lumbar** is an ergonomic lumbar support accessory specifically designed to enhance comfort and support for individuals who spend prolonged periods sitting. This advanced support system is ideal for office chairs and gaming setups, aiming to alleviate back pain by fostering proper posture.

## Features
- **Ergonomic Design:** Tailored to provide optimal support for the mid and lower back, promoting proper posture.
- **Patented TPEE Modules:** These modules dynamically adapt to your body movements, ensuring consistent support.
- **Memory Foam Layer:** Offers added cushioning for enhanced comfort.
- **Breathable Velvet Fabric Cover:** Infused with coffee grounds to regulate temperature and reduce odors.
