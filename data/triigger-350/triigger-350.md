# Triigger 350 Gaming Chair

![Triigger 350](https://www.vertagear.com/images/triigger350.jpg)

The **Triigger 350** is a sophisticated gaming chair meticulously designed for gamers who demand comfort and durability. It supports users up to 350 lbs and 6'6" in height, ensuring a comfortable gaming experience for a wide range of individuals.

## Features

- **Ergonomic Design**: Advanced ergonomic adjustments tailored for extended gaming sessions.
- **Reinforced DuPont TPEE Mesh**: High-quality mesh that withstands high temperatures and hydraulic movements. Offers excellent breathability and prolonged lifespan.
- **Premium Calfskin Leather**: Handcrafted leather with a smooth, luxurious texture that enhances comfort and durability.
- **Aluminum Alloy 5-Star Base**: Provides stability and lightweight construction, ensuring longevity and durability.
- **Class-4 Gas Lift**: Industrial-grade gas lift for optimal height adjustment, reducing strain on your shoulders.
- **Holtron Hub-less Lockable Casters**: 3"/75mm casters with all-metal ball bearings for smooth movement, supporting up to 154 lbs each.
- **Armrest Height Adjustment**: Quick adjustments to seating positions for seamless gaming.
- **Class 12.9 Screws**: High-strength screws for enhanced durability and security.
