# Razer Iskur Line

![Razer Iskur Logo](https://assets2.razerzone.com/images/main-menu/icons/icon-ergonomic-iskur.svg)

The **Razer Iskur Line** is a series of ergonomic gaming chairs crafted to ensure optimal posture and comfort for extended periods of use. These chairs are perfect for gamers and professionals alike, combining functionality with a sleek design.

## Features

- **Ergonomic Design:** Tailored to support the natural curvature of your spine, promoting healthy posture.
- **Adaptive Lumbar Support:** Fully adjustable lumbar support that adapts to your back for personalized comfort.
- **High-Density Foam Cushions:** Provides plush comfort and support for extended seating periods.
- **Sturdy Build:** Constructed with durable materials for long-lasting use.
- **Multi-Layered Synthetic Leather:** Offers a luxurious feel while being easy to maintain.
