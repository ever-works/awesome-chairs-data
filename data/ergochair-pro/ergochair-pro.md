## ErgoChair Pro - Product Overview

![ErgoChair Pro](https://www.autonomous.ai/images/ergopro-chair-1.jpg)

### Description
The **ErgoChair Pro** by **Autonomous** is a versatile ergonomic chair meticulously designed for those who spend long hours seated, such as gamers and office workers. Its design focuses on providing maximum comfort and support through its adjustable features and high-quality materials.

### Features
- **Dynamic Lumbar Support**: Offers customized lower back support to match the natural curve of your spine, reducing discomfort.
- **Adjustable Armrests**: Modify the height, depth, and angle to alleviate shoulder and wrist strain.
- **Breathable Mesh Back**: Ensures ventilation and comfort, even during extended periods of sitting.
- **Adjustable Seat Depth**: Ranges from 19 inches to 21.5 inches to accommodate different leg lengths.
- **Adjustable Headrest**: Allows for neck relief with customizable height and tilt.
- **High-Density Foam Cushion**: Provides a comfortable and supportive seating experience.
- **Reclining Feature**: Facilitates transitions between upright and relaxed positions.
- **Weight Capacity**: Supports up to 300 lbs, catering to a variety of body types.
