# X Rocker Gaming Chairs

**Brand:** X Rocker  
**Category:** Gaming chairs / seating  
**Website:** https://xrockergaming.com/collections/gaming-chairs

## Overview

X Rocker manufactures floor and pedestal-style gaming chairs designed for console and PC gamers who spend long periods seated. Many models integrate built-in audio and vibration systems to enhance in‑game immersion. The range covers different age groups (kids, teens, adults) and use cases, from casual living‑room play to desk‑based PC gaming.

## Features

### Chair Types
- **Floor rockers** – low-profile chairs that sit directly on the floor and rock back and forth.  
- **PC/office gaming chairs** – desk-height chairs suitable for PC setups and office use.

### Integrated Technology (varies by model)
- **Built-in audio options**  
  - Chairs with 2.0 speaker setups (two speakers).  
  - Chairs with 2.1 audio (two speakers plus subwoofer).  
  - Chairs with 4.1 audio (four speakers plus subwoofer).  
- **Vibration** – select chairs incorporate vibration motors synced to audio or game sound for haptic feedback (noted in brand description as “many with built‑in audio and vibration”).
- **LED / RGB lighting (Neo Motion™ range)**  
  - Models featuring integrated LED or RGB lighting effects around the chair.

### Ergonomics & Adjustability (varies by model)
- **3D and 4D armrests** on selected PC/office chairs, providing multi‑directional adjustability (height, depth, pivot, and in some cases lateral movement).
- Designs intended for extended play sessions, with forms tailored to gaming postures.

### Age Ranges
- **Kids gaming chairs** – scaled and styled for younger users.  
- **Teen gaming chairs** – mid-sized with gaming aesthetics.  
- **Adult gaming chairs** – full-size chairs suitable for larger frames.

### Branding & Licensed Collections
- **XR Racing**– racing‑style gaming chair range.  
- **Official PlayStation**– chairs styled and branded to match PlayStation products.  
- **Official Marvel**– themed chairs featuring Marvel branding.
