# Midcelli 2800 Series – Mesh Back Chair

Ergonomic mesh-back midback task chair designed for extended computer and desk use. Combines a breathable mesh back with BodyBilt’s contoured, pressure-reducing seat options to support comfortable, healthy posture during long periods of sitting.

## Features

- **Ergonomic midback design** with a sophisticated, slim profile for task and office workstations.
- **Elastomeric mesh back** engineered for airflow to help keep the user cooler during extended sitting.
- **Targeted sacral and lumbar support** built into the mesh back for lower-back support.
- **Upper back and shoulder support** through a midback frame sized for both lumbar and shoulder regions.
- **Air Cell Lumbar (AL)** system in the backrest for additional lumbar comfort.
- **Multiple seat size options** to better match user body type:
  - Standard Seat (Moderate Contouring) – #7 seat
  - Standard Seat (Flat) – #8 seat
  - Petite (Flat) Seat – #6 seat
- **Seat foam choices** for different comfort and performance needs:
  - High Resiliency Foam (HR)
  - S’port® Foam Seat (SFS)
  - SKYDEX (SKYDEX)
- **Quickship fabric options** (Grade 01):
  - Hue – Black
  - Hue – Indigo
  - Staccato – Navy
  - Staccato – Black
- **Armrest options** (adjustable task arms):
  - Optima Platinum Arms (AOPR/L)
  - Ultra Arms (ARM-AT-LT-RT)
- **Center pivot tilt mechanisms** (slim-profile and standard variants) to allow postural changes:
  - Center Pivot (Slim Profile) (A) Mechanism
  - Center Pivot (J) Mechanism
- **Seat depth adjustment** to help fit different leg lengths.
- **Seat height adjustment** with multiple cylinder ranges to accommodate various user heights.
- **360° swivel** for full rotation and ease of movement.
- **Five-star base** with black finish (B18) for stability.
- **Multi-surface 2.5" casters** (C18) suitable for a range of flooring types.
- **300 lb weight capacity**, suitable for most users.
