## Steelcase Series 1 Work Chair

### Overview
The **Steelcase Series 1 Work Chair** is a budget-friendly ergonomic office chair designed to provide excellent support and comfort for a variety of body types. This chair is ideal for those seeking quality seating solutions without a hefty price tag.

### Features
- **Adjustability**: 
  - **Seat Height**: Pneumatic adjustment mechanism allows for a 5" range.
  - **Seat Depth**: Adjustable within a 2¼" range to accommodate users with different leg lengths.
  - **Lumbar Support**: Height adjustable over a 2¼" range, enhancing lower back support.
  - **Arm Adjustments**: 
    - Arm height adjustable within a 5" range.
    - Arm width can be adjusted by 4" overall.
    - Arm depth can retract 2" for closer proximity to the work surface.
    - Arm caps pivot 40° inward and outward.
- **Back Support**: 
  - **Comfort Dial**: Offers three settings - weight-activated, boosted, and upright back lock for personalized comfort.
  - **3D Microknit Back**: Provides breathable support.
- **Casters and Mobility**: 
  - Dual-wheel casters for smooth rolling on carpets.
  - Optional soft casters available for hard floors or chair mats.
- **Base and Frame**:
  - Five-star base available in paint, plastic, or polished aluminum.
