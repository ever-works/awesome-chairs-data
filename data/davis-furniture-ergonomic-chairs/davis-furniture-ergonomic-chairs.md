## Davis Furniture Ergonomic Chairs

### Overview
Davis Furniture Ergonomic Chairs are customizable office and task seating solutions designed for long-term comfort and support in computer‑intensive and other extended‑sitting work environments. Davis Furniture is an American manufacturer established in 1944 and headquartered in North Carolina, USA.

### Features
- **Ergonomic design for extended use**  
  - Chairs are designed to provide long‑term comfort and support.  
  - Suitable for computer‑intensive work and other tasks requiring users to sit for extended periods.

- **Chair types and applications**  
  - Office chairs for general workplace use.  
  - Task chairs aimed at focused, desk‑based work.  
  - Visitor chairs appropriate for guests or meeting areas.  
  - Training chairs suitable for classrooms, seminar rooms, or collaborative learning spaces.  
  - Leather office chair options for executive or premium environments.

- **Customization and finishes**  
  - More than **750 fabric options** available.  
  - Over **60 different finishes** to coordinate with various interiors and branding requirements.  
  - Configurable across the product line to match aesthetic and functional needs.

- **Intended environments**  
  - Offices and corporate workspaces.  
  - Training and education rooms.  
  - Visitor and reception areas.  
  - Any setting where users remain seated for long durations.
