### Description
The **Eurotech Ryze Task chair** is a premier ergonomic seating solution designed to offer maximum comfort and support. - **Adjustable Center-Tilt Mechanism**: Allows for a customized seating angle.
- **Pneumatic Height Adjustment**: Easily change the chair's height for optimal comfort.
- **Flip-Up Armrests**: Provides flexibility for arm positioning or can be moved out of the way when not needed.
- **Sleek Aesthetic**: Modern design that complements contemporary workspaces.
