## Description

The **Classic 3500 Series Executive Back Chair** by BodyBilt is engineered for comfort and ergonomics, inspired by NASA technology. It is designed to alleviate back and leg pain through its unique pressure-reducing contoured seat and back. Perfect for those who spend long hours seated, this chair supports a sleek and professional look.

## Features

- **Ergonomic Design:** 
  - Proprietary pressure-reducing contoured seat and back
  - Dual Pivot Neckroll for added neck support
- **Customization Options:** 
  - Multiple seat sizes, including options for standard and big & tall users
  - Variety of quickship fabrics
  - Optional air lumbar support
  - Foam choices including High Resiliency Foam, S’port® Foam, and SKYDEX
- **Adjustability:**
  - Backrest angle adjustment of 12°
  - Backrest height adjustment of 4.00"
  - Lumbar height adjustment of 3.50"
  - Seat depth adjustment of 2.50" to 3.50"
  - Seat height range from 17.00" to 20.50"
- **Construction and Durability:**
  - Optima Platinum Arms
  - Center Pivot (J) Mechanism
  - Black Cylinder options for varying heights
  - Marquis Base in 26" or 27" diameter
  - Standard Carpet Casters
- **Weight Capacity:**
  - Options for 300 lbs and 500 lbs
