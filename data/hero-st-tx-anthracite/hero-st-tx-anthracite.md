# HERO ST TX Anthracite

![HERO ST TX Anthracite](https://www.noblechairs.com/images/hero-st-tx-anthracite.jpg)

## Overview
The **HERO ST TX Anthracite** is a sophisticated gaming chair from the HERO series, renowned for its ergonomic design and high-quality materials. Its unique cutout design and anthracite color make it a standout choice for gamers seeking both style and comfort.

## Features

- **Breathable Fabric**: Ensures comfort during extended gaming sessions by promoting airflow.
- **Ergonomic Design**: Supports proper posture and reduces fatigue with its ergonomic structure.
- **Adjustable Lumbar Support**: Customizable support to enhance back comfort.
- **4D Armrests**: Fully adjustable armrests that move in four directions for personalized comfort.
- **Cold-Foam Upholstery**: Durable and maintains shape over time, providing consistent support.
- **XL Casters**: Large, smooth-rolling casters that support up to 150 kg, offering mobility and stability.
