## Overview
The **Sihoo M18** is an ergonomic office chair designed to provide maximum comfort and support throughout the workday. Its high backrest and adjustable headrest make it suitable for various sitting positions, catering to users who require adaptability in their seating arrangement.

## Features
- **High Backrest:** Offers comprehensive support for the spine and back, enhancing comfort during extended periods of sitting.
- **Adjustable Headrest:** Customizable head support to suit different user preferences and sitting postures.
- **Ergonomic Design:** Tailored to fit the natural curvature of the spine, promoting healthy posture and reducing strain.
- **Soft Cushion:** Provides a comfortable seat for prolonged use, ensuring minimal discomfort even after hours of sitting.
- **Spine Support:** Features triple spinal relief to alleviate pressure on the spine, improving overall seating ergonomics.
