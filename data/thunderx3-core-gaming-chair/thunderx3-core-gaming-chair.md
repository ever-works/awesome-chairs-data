## ThunderX3 Core Gaming Chair

![ThunderX3 Core Gaming Chair](https://example.com/thunderx3-core-image1.jpg)

### Overview
The **ThunderX3 Core Gaming Chair** is a premium seating solution for avid gamers and professionals who spend extensive time seated. With its racing seat design and robust ergonomic features, it ensures comfort and support during long gaming sessions.

### Features
- **Racing Seat Design:** Inspired by car racing seats, the chair offers a sleek and sporty look.
- **Recline Range:** Adjustable backrest with a recline range from 90 to 135 degrees for personalized comfort.
- **Weight Capacity:** Supports up to 150 kg (331 lbs), accommodating a wide range of users.
- **Ergonomic Mechanisms:** Designed to provide optimal back support and reduce strain during prolonged sitting.
- **Easy Assembly:** User-friendly assembly process to get you seated quickly.
- **Competitive Pricing:** Offers high-end features at a value-for-money price point.
