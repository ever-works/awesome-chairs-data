## Overview
Humanscale World One is an all‑mesh ergonomic task chair designed as a lower‑cost, lightweight option for daily office and computer work. It uses a self‑adjusting recline mechanism with very few manual controls, focusing on simplicity rather than fine‑tuned adjustability. Reviews mention that it may be better suited to shorter users, with some compromises in materials feel and reclined comfort.

## Features
- **All‑mesh construction** for seat and backrest.
- **Ergonomic task chair** intended for all‑day computer and office use.
- **Self‑adjusting recline** mechanism that automatically adapts to the user rather than relying on multiple tension knobs.
- **Minimal manual controls**, primarily:
  - Seat height adjustment
  - Armrest height adjustment
- **Lightweight design** compared with many traditional task chairs.
- **Positioning as a lower‑cost option** within Humanscale’s seating lineup.
- **Fit characteristics (based on review notes):**
  - Likely more suitable for shorter users
  - Taller users may find the fit less supportive.
- **Comfort considerations from reviews:**
  - Reclining comfort reported as limited
  - Build quality described as somewhat plasticky compared with higher‑end models.
