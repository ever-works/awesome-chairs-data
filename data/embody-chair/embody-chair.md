**Brand:** Herman Miller

## Overview
The Embody Chair is an ergonomic work chair designed for people who sit for extended periods. It uses a pixelated support system that adapts to the user’s body shape and posture changes, providing support for the sacral, lumbar, and thoracic regions while helping dissipate heat for all‑day comfort at a computer.

## Features
- **Ergonomic design for long sitting sessions**
  - Intended for intensive computer and desk work
  - Promotes healthy posture over extended periods

- **Pixelated support system**
  - Conforms to the user’s body shape
  - Responds to postural movements in real time
  - Distributes pressure to help reduce hotspots

- **Multi‑zone back support**
  - Sacral support
  - Lumbar support
  - Thoracic (upper back) support

- **Adjustable seat depth**
  - Range: 15"–18"
  - Helps accommodate different leg lengths

- **Recline and tilt control**
  - Deep, balanced recline mechanism
  - Tilt limiter to set maximum recline angle

- **Arm options**
  - Optional fully adjustable arms (height and position)

- **Backrest design for mobility**
  - Frameless back to allow a wide range of movement
  - Narrow back shape to free arms, neck, and shoulders for reach and rotation

- **Thermal comfort**
  - Construction promotes heat dissipation for all‑day comfort

- **Performance and safety standards**
  - Meets ANSI/BIFMA X5.1‑2017 office chair standards
  - Meets California TB 117‑2013
  - Tested and warranted for users up to 300 lb (136 kg)
