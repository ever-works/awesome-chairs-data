# Ergohuman High Back Swivel Chair

![Ergohuman Logo](https://www.ergohuman.com/images/logo.png)

## Overview
The **Ergohuman High Back Swivel Chair** is designed to provide ergonomic support and comfort for prolonged sitting sessions. It blends style with functionality, making it an excellent choice for office environments.

## Features
- **High Back Design**: Ensures full back support, essential for maintaining a healthy posture during long hours of sitting.
- **Adjustable Lumbar Support**: Allows users to customize the chair's support to fit their specific needs, reducing strain on the lower back.
- **Headrest**: Provides additional support for the neck and head, which can be adjusted for optimal comfort.
- **Adjustable Armrests**: These can be moved to suit individual preferences, helping to alleviate shoulder tension.
- **Swivel Functionality**: Enhances mobility and accessibility within a workspace, allowing for ease of movement.
