## Product Overview
The **ICON TX Anthracite** chair from **noblechairs** is a blend of subtle style and superior functionality, perfect for enhancing any gaming or office setup. Designed with ergonomic precision, it ensures comfort and support for extended periods of use.

## Features
- **Stylish Design**: The chair features a sleek and modern look that fits seamlessly into any setting.
- **Breathable Fabric**: Ensures comfort by allowing air circulation, reducing heat build-up during long sessions.
- **Superior Ergonomics**: Designed to support healthy posture, reducing strain on your back and neck.
- **4D Armrests**: Adjustable armrests that can be customized in four directions to suit your sitting preference.
- **60 mm Castors**: Equipped with durable castors that provide smooth movement across different floor surfaces.
- **Head and Lumbar Pillow Set**: Includes supportive pillows to enhance comfort and reduce fatigue.
