## Overview
The **BodyBilt Sola LT Chair** is an ergonomically designed chair aimed at providing comfort and support for prolonged sitting periods. It is highly customizable to meet individual needs, making it suitable for both home and office settings.

- **Customizable Options:** Multiple adjustment settings to cater to individual preferences and requirements.
- **Versatile Use:** Ideal for both home offices and professional workspaces.
