# Anthros V2 Ergonomic Office Chair

**Category:** Chair & Accessories (Ergonomic Office Chair)  
**Brand:** Anthros  
**Website:** <https://www.anthros.com/>

## Description
The Anthros V2 is a high-end ergonomic office chair designed for people who spend long hours at a computer. It emphasizes posture support and all-day comfort through an adjustable back and seat structure intended to reduce strain during extended work or gaming sessions.

## Features
- High-end ergonomic design for intensive computer use
- Adjustable back structure to support healthy posture
- Adjustable seat structure for customized fit and comfort
- Focus on reducing physical strain during long sitting periods
- Suitable for both office work and gaming scenarios
- Designed for all-day comfort and posture support
