## AndaSeat Kaiser 3E Series

![AndaSeat Logo](/images/andaseat-logo.png)

### Overview
The AndaSeat Kaiser 3E Series is designed to provide ergonomic support for both office and gaming environments. With its durable construction and adjustable features, it ensures comfort and stability for long hours of use.

### Features
- **Ergonomic Design**: The chair includes a 5cm raised lumbar support to maintain proper posture.
- **4D Armrests**: These adjustable armrests offer flexibility in height, depth, width, and angle.
- **Durable Construction**: Built with aluminum and iron wheelbases for enhanced stability and durability.
- **Cold-Cure Foam Seat**: Ensures comfort with its high-density foam padding.
- **Wide and Deep Seat**: Provides ample space for users of various sizes.
