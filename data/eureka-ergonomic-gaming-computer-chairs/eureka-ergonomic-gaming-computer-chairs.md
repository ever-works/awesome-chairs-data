## Eureka Ergonomic Gaming & Computer Chairs

**Category:** chair-accessories  
**Brand:** Eureka Ergonomic  
**Listing type:** Product line / collection  
**Official page:** https://eurekaergonomic.com/collections/gaming-chairs

A collection of ergonomic computer and gaming chairs designed for people who sit for extended periods. These chairs prioritize spinal support and adjustability to help reduce fatigue during long PC work or gaming sessions.

---

## Features

- **Ergonomic design for long sessions**  
  Designed specifically for users who sit for long hours at a computer or gaming setup.

- **Lumbar support**  
  Built-in lower back support to encourage healthier posture and reduce lower-back strain.

- **Neck and head support**  
  Upper-back and neck support to help reduce neck fatigue during extended use.

- **Adjustable reclining mechanism**  
  Backrest can recline to multiple angles so users can switch between upright work and more relaxed positions.

- **Rocking function**  
  Rocking mechanisms allow gentle movement rather than remaining in a fixed position.

- **Padded armrests**  
  Cushioned armrests to support forearms and reduce shoulder and wrist fatigue.

- **Hybrid / gaming-office use**  
  Models are presented as “hybrid” ergonomic or gaming chairs, suitable for both gaming and general computer/office work.

- **Contract-grade construction**  
  Labeled as contract grade, indicating build quality intended for more demanding or frequent use environments.
