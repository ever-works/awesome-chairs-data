#### Overview
Eureka Ergonomic Gaming & Computer Chairs are designed for users who sit for extended periods, such as PC users and gamers. The collection focuses on ergonomic support for the lumbar and neck areas, with adjustability and cushioning intended to reduce fatigue over long sessions.

---

#### Features
- Ergonomic design for long-duration sitting (PC use and gaming)
- Emphasis on lumbar support
- Neck support / headrest support
- Adjustable reclining mechanisms
- Rocking functionality
- Padded armrests for added comfort
- Suitable for extended gaming or computer work sessions
- Contract-grade construction across the listed models
- Multiple color options available per model (varies by chair)
