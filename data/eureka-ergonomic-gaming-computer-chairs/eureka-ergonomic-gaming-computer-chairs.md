## Overview
Eureka Ergonomic offers a collection of gaming and computer chairs designed for people who sit for extended periods, such as PC users and gamers. The chairs are focused on ergonomics, aiming to support the spine and reduce fatigue during long sessions.

## Features
- **Ergonomic construction for long sessions** – Designed specifically for users who sit for many hours at a time.
- **Lumbar support** – Built-in back support intended to help maintain a healthier posture and reduce lower-back strain.
- **Neck support** – Headrest or neck-support components to help relieve tension in the upper spine and neck.
- **Adjustable reclining mechanism** – Backrest angle can be adjusted to suit different sitting and resting positions.
- **Rocking function** – Rocking capability for dynamic sitting and periodic movement.
- **Padded armrests** – Cushioning on the armrests to reduce pressure on forearms and shoulders during extended use.
- **Focus on fatigue reduction** – Overall design (support + adjustability + padding) aimed at lowering fatigue during prolonged computer work or gaming.
