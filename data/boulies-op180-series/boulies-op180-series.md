# Boulies OP180 Series

**Brand:** boulies  
**Category:** Office chair  
**Website:** <https://boulies.com>  
**Featured:** Yes

## Overview
The Boulies OP180 Series is an ergonomic office and computer chair range designed for long sessions of desk work or gaming (around 8–10 hours of continuous sitting). It focuses on posture flexibility, including forward-tilt seating, to help maintain circulation and reduce fatigue during extended use.

## Features
- **Ergonomic design for long hours**: Built for approximately 8–10 hours of continuous sitting.
- **Posture flexibility**:
  - Supports multiple sitting postures.
  - Includes forward tilt to encourage active sitting and better circulation.
- **Dynamic lumbar support**: Back support adjusts with posture changes to help maintain spinal alignment.
- **Hybrid leather upholstery**: Combines materials for a balance of comfort, durability, and easy maintenance.
- **Multi-tilt mechanism**: Adjustable reclining and tilt functions for customized seating angle and tension.
- **4D adjustable armrests**: Armrests adjustable in multiple directions (height, depth, width, and pivot) to support different body types and desk setups.
- **Use cases**: Suitable for general office work, home office setups, and gaming.
