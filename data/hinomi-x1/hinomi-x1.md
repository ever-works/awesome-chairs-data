# Hinomi X1

## Overview
The Hinomi X1 is a high-end mesh ergonomic computer chair designed for people who spend long hours working or gaming. It combines a firm, supportive seat with a breathable mesh design and extensive adjustability for posture support and comfort.

## Features
- **Ergonomic mesh design** for breathability and temperature regulation during long sitting sessions
- **Firm, supportive seat** to maintain comfort and posture over extended use
- **Built-in extendable footrest** for leg support and reclining comfort
- **Adjustable lumbar support** to better fit different body shapes and lower-back needs
- **Multiple ergonomic adjustments** (e.g., likely seat, back, and arm adjustments) to fine-tune sitting position
- **Suitable for long work or gaming sessions**, designed for users who sit many hours per day
- **Long warranty (up to 10 years)** for long-term durability and reassurance
