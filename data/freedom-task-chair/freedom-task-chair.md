## Overview  
The **Freedom Task Chair** by **Humanscale** is an ergonomic office chair designed to enhance comfort and support for individuals who spend extended periods seated at a desk. This chair aims to promote healthy posture and reduce fatigue through its innovative design and functional features.

## Features
- **Weight-Sensitive Recline:** Automatically adjusts to the user's weight, offering a balanced recline experience without the need for manual adjustments.
- **Synchronous Armrests:** Move in tandem with the seat back, providing continuous support for the arms.
- **Dynamic Headrest:** Adjusts to the user's movements, ensuring support and comfort for the neck and head.
- **Ergonomic Design:** Crafted to promote a healthy sitting posture, reducing strain and enhancing comfort.
