## HERO ST Black Edition

![HERO ST Black Edition](https://www.noblechairs.com/images/hero-st-black-edition-1.jpg)

The **HERO ST Black Edition** by **noblechairs** is a premium gaming chair crafted with high-tech faux leather, designed to provide comfort and support for gamers and office workers alike. This chair is not just about looks; it's engineered for ergonomic comfort and durability.

### Features:

- **Ergonomic Design**: Tailored to support your posture during long gaming or work sessions.
- **Adjustable Lumbar Support**: Customizable to fit the natural curve of your spine.
- **4D Armrests**: Offers multidirectional adjustability for arm support.
- **Cold-Foam Upholstery**: Ensures long-lasting comfort and breathability.
- **High Weight Capacity**: Supports up to 150 kg (330 lbs).
