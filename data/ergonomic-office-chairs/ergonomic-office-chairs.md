## Overview
ErgoChairs Inc. offers a variety of ergonomic office chairs designed to provide maximum comfort and support for individuals who spend extended periods sitting at a desk. These chairs are crafted to improve posture and alleviate strain on the back and neck, making them ideal for both professional office environments and gaming setups.

## Features
- **Ergonomic Design**: Engineered to support posture and reduce physical strain, promoting a healthier sitting experience.
- **Adjustable Components**: Includes adjustable armrests, seat height, and recline settings to accommodate various body types and preferences.
- **Lumbar Support**: Integrated lumbar support to maintain the natural curve of the spine, reducing back pain.
- **Durable Build**: Made from high-quality materials to ensure longevity and durability.
- **Versatile Use**: Suitable for both office work and gaming setups, providing comfort during long hours of use.
