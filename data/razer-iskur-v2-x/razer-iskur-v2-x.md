# Razer Iskur V2 X: Gaming Chair Overview

The **Razer Iskur V2 X** is an ergonomically designed gaming chair that aims to deliver essential comfort features at an affordable price. Ideal for gamers who prioritize both comfort and budget, this chair is packed with features to enhance your gaming experience.

## Features

- **Integrated Lumbar Support**: The chair is equipped with a built-in lumbar curve designed to support your lower back and promote a neutral sitting posture, reducing back fatigue over long gaming sessions.

- **Wider Seat Base**: The seat base is sculpted with reduced edges, offering a wider surface area that ensures better pressure distribution and greater flexibility in seating positions.

- **2D Armrests**: The armrests can be adjusted in height and rotated either inwards or outwards, ensuring that arms are always positioned naturally and comfortably.

- **Breathable Multi-Layered Fabric**: Upholstered in a plush fabric that is both breathable and smooth to the touch, available in Black and Light Gray.
