## Features

- **Ergonomic design** tailored for people who spend long hours seated at a workstation.

- **Breathable back structure** to enhance airflow and comfort during extended sitting.
- **Distinctive visual design** with an open, patterned back frame.
- **Multiple configuration options** shown in images (different colors, finishes, and upholstery combinations).
- **Upholstery choices** including:

- **Color variety** demonstrated in product photos (white, black, blue, green, tan, magenta and other accent colors).
