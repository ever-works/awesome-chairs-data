#### Description
The X-Chair X2 K-Sport Management Chair is an ergonomic mesh office and computer chair designed for extended daily use. It features extensive adjustability, dynamic lumbar support that moves with the user, and a breathable mesh seat and back that provide a suspended, hammock-like feel. It is suitable for taller users and for intensive computer or gaming use.

---

#### Features
- **Ergonomic design**
  - Designed for all-day sitting in office, home office, or gaming setups
  - Supports intensive computer use
  - Suitable for taller users

- **Seat and back**
  - Mesh construction for breathability
  - Hammock-like, suspended feel for even weight distribution

- **Lumbar and back support**
  - Dynamic lumbar support that adjusts as you move and change posture

- **Adjustability**
  - Adjustable seat height
  - Adjustable armrests, including:
    - Height (up and down)
    - Width (side to side)
    - Angle (arm caps can be angled/rotated)
  - Overall configuration tailored to ergonomic sitting positions

- **Construction & use**
  - Built for frequent, long-duration use
  - Designed for durability under daily office or gaming conditions

- **Wheel / caster options** (chair-compatible options shown on site)
  - **Standard casters**
    - Suitable for most floor types
    - Provide basic, reliable mobility
  - **FS Wheel Non-Locking**
    - Dual-bearing design for smooth, quiet rolling
    - Suitable for use on a variety of surfaces
    - Adds approximately 1" to seat height
  - **FS Wheel Locking**
    - Rollerblade-style casters
    - Can be locked to keep the chair in place when desired
    - Adds approximately 1" to seat height
