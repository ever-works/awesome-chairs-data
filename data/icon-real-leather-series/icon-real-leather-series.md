## ICON Real Leather Series by noblechairs

![noblechairs Logo](https://www.noblechairs.com/logo.png)

### Overview
The **ICON Real Leather Series** by noblechairs is a luxurious gaming chair designed for comfort and style. It features high-quality materials and ergonomic design, making it a perfect addition to any gaming setup or office space.

### Features
- **Material:** Constructed with top grain leather in an elegant cognac/black color, providing a premium look and feel.
- **Comfort:** Cold foam upholstery ensures long-lasting comfort and support during extended sitting periods.
- **Ergonomics:** Engineered for superior ergonomics to promote healthy posture and reduce strain, accommodating weights of up to 150 kg.
- **Durability:** Built with high-quality materials for durability and longevity, ensuring that it withstands the test of time.
