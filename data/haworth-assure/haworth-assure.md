## Haworth Assure Office Chair

![Haworth Logo](/path/to/haworth-logo.png)

### Overview
The **Haworth Assure** is designed for the modern workday, offering superior comfort and adjustability for those who spend long hours at their desks. Known for its ergonomic features, it is a reliable choice for anyone seeking a durable and comfortable seating solution.

### Features
- **Ergonomic Design**: 
  - Center tension synchro-tilt mechanism with an upright back lock.
  - Adjustable arm height.
  - 3" seat height adjustment.
  - Single-position back lock.
  - Mid-back profile.
- **Comfort**:
  - Generous seat cushion.
  - Molded seat conforms to your body.
  - Plenty of lumbar support.
- **Durability**: 
  - 5-year warranty.
  - Warrantied for people up to 300 lbs.
