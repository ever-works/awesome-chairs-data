## Libernovo Omni: Product Summary

### Overview
The **Libernovo Omni** is an advanced computer chair engineered for individuals who require comfort and ergonomic support during prolonged sitting periods. Ideal for professionals like engineers, designers, and gamers, this chair integrates innovative features to enhance comfort and reduce discomfort commonly associated with extended sitting.

### Features
- **Innovative Back Stretch Mechanism**: Allows users to lean back and activate a piston-driven stretching mode for added relaxation and comfort.
- **3D Adjustable Neck Support**: Provides zero-pressure support to alleviate cervical strain and neck stiffness.
- **FlexFit BackRest**: Equipped with 14 dual-connection points to seamlessly conform to the spine, dynamically easing back and shoulder tension.
- **Multi-Density Cushion**: Firm rear supports sit bones, while a softer front cushion reduces leg pressure and enhances circulation.
- **4D, 8-Way Adjustable Armrests**: Offers precise adjustments to alleviate postural fatigue and ensure personalized comfort.
- **OmniStretch and Motorized Spinal Massage**: Adjustable between 105° and 160° across four positions, perfect for work, gaming, or unwinding, providing relief from muscle fatigue and disc issues.

### Benefits
- **Neck Stiffness Relief**: The 3D-adjustable neck support provides gentle cradling.
- **Back and Shoulder Tension Reduction**: The FlexFit BackRest conforms to the spine and promotes all-day comfort.
- **Improved Circulation and Hip Comfort**: Multi-density cushion supports extended, pain-free sitting.
- **Postural Fatigue and Muscle Tension Alleviation**: Adjustable armrests and backrest settings enhance comfort and reduce strain.
