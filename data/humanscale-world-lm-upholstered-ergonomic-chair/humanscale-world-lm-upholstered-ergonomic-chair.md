## Overview
The **Humanscale World LM Upholstered Ergonomic Chair** is crafted to deliver exceptional comfort and support for prolonged sitting periods. With its modern and sleek design, this chair is an excellent choice for both office and home environments.

## Features
- **Ergonomic Design**: Engineered with a tri-panel mesh backrest that adapts to the user's body for optimal spine support.
- **Weight-Sensitive Recline**: Automatically adjusts to the user's weight, offering a seamless and personalized recline experience.
- **Upholstered Seat and Back**: Provides a plush and stylish seating solution.
- **Adjustable Duron Arms**: Ensures customizable arm support to suit individual preferences.
- **Variety of Mesh Options**: Available in diverse colors such as Cornflower, Bayberry, and Caraway.
- **Streamlined Edition**: A refined version of the original World chair, maintaining its core ergonomic principles.
