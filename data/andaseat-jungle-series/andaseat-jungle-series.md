# AndaSeat Jungle Series

![AndaSeat Jungle Series](https://www.andaseat.com/images/jungle-1.png)

## Overview
The AndaSeat Jungle Series is a gaming chair that combines ergonomic design with affordability, making it an ideal choice for those who spend long hours sitting, whether for work or play. Built with a robust steel frame and high-density foam, this chair promises both durability and comfort.

## Features
- **Ergonomic Design:** The chair offers adjustable armrests and lumbar support to promote healthy posture and reduce strain during prolonged sitting.
- **Durability:** Constructed with a solid steel frame and a 5-star aluminum base, ensuring long-lasting use.
- **Comfort:** Features high-density memory foam cushions, with detachable lumbar and neck support pillows for additional comfort.
- **Material:** Made with PVC Leather, which is both hard-wearing and easy to clean.
- **Weight Capacity:** Supports a maximum load of 22 stone (approximately 308 lbs), making it suitable for larger users.
- **Easy Assembly:** Comes with clear instructions and pre-installed bolts for a straightforward assembly process.
