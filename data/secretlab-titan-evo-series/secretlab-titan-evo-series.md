## Secretlab Titan Evo Series

![Secretlab Titan Evo Series](https://secretlab.co/images/titan-evo-2025-front.png)

### Overview
The **Secretlab Titan Evo Series** is a high-end gaming chair designed to provide comfort and support during long hours of sitting. It is ideal for gamers and professionals who require ergonomic seating solutions.

### Features
- **Ergonomic Design**: Proprietary pebble seat base for optimal support.
- **Material Options**:
  - **Secretlab SoftWeave™ Plus Fabric**: Enhanced breathability and durability.
  - **Secretlab NEO™ Hybrid Leatherette**: Luxurious feel, 12x more durable than standard PU leather.
- **Lumbar Support**: 4-way L-ADAPT™ Lumbar Support System for customizable back support.
- **Armrests**: Full-metal 4D Armrests with CloudSwap™ Replacement System.
- **Head Pillow**: Patented Magnetic Memory Foam Head Pillow for added comfort.
- **Recline and Tilt**:
  - Full-length backrest with 165° of recline.
  - Multi-tilt mechanism for enhanced flexibility.
- **Hydraulics**: Shorter, re-engineered Class 4 Hydraulics for improved stability.
- **Wheelbase and Casters**: ADC12 Aluminum Wheelbase with XL PU casters for smooth movement.
- **Warranty**: Up to a 5-year Extended Warranty available.
- **Anti-Counterfeit**: NFC anti-counterfeit tag for authenticity verification.
- **Foam**: Patent-pending cold-cure foam mix for even weight distribution and pressure relief.

### Sizes
- **Small**: Suitable for individuals ≤5’6” and <200lbs.
- **Regular**: Suitable for individuals 5’7”-6’2” and <220lbs.
- **XL**: Suitable for individuals 5’11”-6’9” and 175lbs-395lbs.
