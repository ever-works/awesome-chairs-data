# Sitmatic GoodFit High & Wide Back Chair

![Sitmatic Logo](/path/to/sitmatic/logo.png)

**Overview:**
The Sitmatic GoodFit High & Wide Back Chair is crafted for individuals who need a larger seating area and enhanced back support. This chair is suitable for both office and gaming settings, offering durability and comfort for prolonged use.

**Features:**
- **High & Wide Backrest:** Provides ample back support, accommodating users who require a larger back area.
- **Large Seat:** Designed to offer spacious seating, promoting comfort during extended sitting periods.
- **Ergonomic Design:** Engineered to support proper posture and reduce strain.
- **Customizable Arm Options:**
  - **7K Arm Option:**
    - Arm height adjusts into 10 different positions.
    - Arm width adjusts into 6 different positions.
    - Soft Gel Caps for comfort.
  - **1A or 1N Arm Option:**
    - Adjustable height, width, and angle.
    - Contoured Urethane Caps.
    - Option to disable arm rotation with 1N.
- **Additional Features:**
  - Inflatable Lumbar Bolster for extra lower back support.
  - Tailbone Seat Cutout to reduce pressure on the coccyx.
  - Memory Foam Seat Topper for enhanced cushioning.
  - Optional Seat Slider with OT Mechanism for adjustable seat depth.

**Quality Assurance:**
- **No Weight Limits:** Built with steel frames, ensuring robustness and reliability for users of all sizes.
- **Lifetime Component Warranty:** Assurance of quality and durability, showcasing Sitmatic's commitment to ergonomic excellence.
