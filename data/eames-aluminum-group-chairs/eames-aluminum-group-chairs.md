## Overview

Eames Aluminum Group Chairs are a classic series of office and lounge chairs designed by Charles and Ray Eames and produced by Herman Miller. They use a tensioned, upholstered seat and back suspended within a sculpted aluminum frame, providing a slim profile suitable for formal executive settings or refined home workspaces where users may sit for long periods.

---

- **Tensioned Upholstered Seat & Back**  
  - Fabric or leather upholstery stretched between frame elements  
  - Creates a firm yet flexible sitting surface for extended use

- **Aluminum Frame Construction**  
  - Exposed cast aluminum side ribs and base  
  - Lightweight yet rigid structure  
  - Signature Eames Aluminum Group silhouette

- **Multiple Chair Types (Series)**  
  - Management / mid-back office chairs  
  - High-back executive chairs  
  - Lounge / low lounge variants

- **Ergonomic Office Use**  
  - Designed for computer workspaces and executive offices  
  - Intended for users who sit for extended periods

- **Refined Aesthetic**

- **Base & Orientation**  
  - Swivel office-chair configurations shown

- **Finish & Color Options**
