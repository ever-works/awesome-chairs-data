# Eurotech Vera Chair

![Eurotech Vera Chair](https://example.com/eurotech-vera-image1.jpg)

## Overview
The Eurotech Vera Chair is a versatile and ergonomic office chair designed to offer premium comfort and support, making it a great addition to any workspace.

## Features
- **Frameless Mesh Back:** Adapts to your back, providing lumbar support and preventing frame contact for enhanced comfort.
- **Synchro Tilt Mechanism:** Allows for a smooth recline, promoting ergonomic posture.
- **6-Way Adjustable Arms:** Customize armrest position for personalized comfort.
- **Adjustable Foam Seat:** Offers adjustable depth and height to accommodate various body types.
- **GREENGUARD Certified:** Ensures low chemical emissions, promoting healthier indoor air quality.
- **Durable Build:** Premium quality materials with a lifetime warranty on components and a 5-year warranty on upholstery and foam.
- **Weight Capacity:** Supports up to 275 lbs.

## Specifications
- **Seat Size:** 18.9" W x 15.75" D
- **Seat Height:** 18.89" - 23.62" H
- **Back Size:** 17.1" W x 20.87" H
- **Armrest Height:** 7.25" - 10" H
- **Overall Dimensions:** 28" D x 28" W x 38.9" - 43.4" H
