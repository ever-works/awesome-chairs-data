## Sihoo M102C Ergonomic Office Chair

### Description
The **Sihoo M102C** is an ergonomic office chair crafted to enhance comfort and improve posture for individuals who spend long hours sitting. It reflects Sihoo's dedication to delivering ergonomic solutions tailored for modern workspaces.

### Features
- **Customizable Lumbar Support**: Designed to provide optimal support for the lower back, reducing discomfort and promoting healthy posture.
- **Adjustability**: Users can modify seat height, armrest positions, and the tilt angle to suit personal preferences, enhancing comfort and adaptability.
- **Ergonomic Design**: Crafted with a focus on human anatomy, ensuring that the chair supports the natural curve of the spine and reduces strain during prolonged seating.
