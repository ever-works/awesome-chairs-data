## Overview
The **Footrest Series GT905** by Ace is a gaming chair designed with gamers in mind. It combines comfort and ergonomic design, featuring a tech fabric finish and pocket spring cushion, which enhances the gaming experience during long sessions.

- **Tech Fabric Material:** Provides a sleek look and feel, ensuring durability and comfort.
- **Pocket Spring Cushion:** Offers superior comfort, adapting to the user's body for prolonged sitting.
- **Integrated Footrest:** Enhances relaxation by allowing gamers to stretch out during breaks.
- **Ergonomic Design:** Supports posture and reduces strain during extended gaming or working hours.
