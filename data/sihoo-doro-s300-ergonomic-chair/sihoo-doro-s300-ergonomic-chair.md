## Overview
The **Sihoo Doro S300 Ergonomic Chair** is crafted to enhance comfort and support for any workspace. It integrates essential ergonomic features to promote health and posture, making it suitable for extended periods of use.

- **Brand:** Sihoo  
- **Category:** Ergonomic Office Chairs  
- **Featured:** Yes  

## Features
- **Ergonomic Design:** Tailored to support the natural curve of the spine, promoting better posture.
- **Adjustable Armrests:** Customize the armrest height and angle for enhanced comfort.
- **Seat Depth Adjustment:** Allows for personalized seating comfort, accommodating various body types.
- **Lumbar Support:** Provides targeted support to the lower back, reducing strain.
- **Breathable Material:** Ensures airflow to keep you cool and comfortable during long hours of use.
- **Stylish Aesthetics:** A futuristic design that complements modern workspaces.
