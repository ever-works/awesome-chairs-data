# Tempur-Pedic Tempur-Lumbar Support Office Chair

A task/office chair designed for all-day sitting comfort, combining TEMPUR-Material™ cushioning with adjustable ergonomic features and a breathable mesh back.

## Features

- **TEMPUR-Material™ Seat Cushion**  
  - Premium TEMPUR® cushion in the seat.  
  - Conforms to body shape and evenly distributes weight.  
  - Provides pressure relief by reducing pressure points.  
  - Responsive to body temperature and weight, then returns to original shape when you stand.

- **TEMPUR® Lumbar Support**  
  - Built-in lumbar cushion made with adaptive TEMPUR-Material™.  
  - Designed to support the lower back and promote a healthier seated posture.

- **Ergonomic Adjustability**  
  - **Height adjustment:** Lever-controlled seat height adjustment up to 4".  
  - **Tilt & tension:** Adjustable tilt of the breathable mesh back up to 7° with tension control.  
  - **Tilt lock:** Ability to lock the chair angle at a preferred recline position.  
  - **360° swivel:** Full rotation for ease of movement.  
  - **Adjustable armrests:** Height/position adjustable arm rests to better match desk setup.

- **Comfort-Oriented Design**  
  - Breathable mesh back to improve airflow while sitting.  
  - TEMPUR® seat and lumbar cushioning for long-duration sitting comfort.  
  - Built-in ergonomics intended to help maintain a healthy seated position throughout the day.

- **Aesthetic & Customization Options**  
  - Choice of different cushion colors to complement home or office decor.

- **Build & Components**  
  - Premium wheels for smooth rolling on typical office surfaces.

- **Health & Environment**  
  - Tested and evaluated by UL to meet stringent chemical emissions limits.

- **Assembly**  
  - Designed for easy assembly with step-by-step instructions.
