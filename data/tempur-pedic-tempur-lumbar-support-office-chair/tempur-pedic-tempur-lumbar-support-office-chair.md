# Tempur-Pedic Tempur-Lumbar Support Office Chair

## Overview
An ergonomic office and computer chair that uses TEMPUR-Material™ in the seat and lumbar areas to provide conforming cushioning and pressure relief for all‑day sitting. It combines a breathable mesh back with a substantial lumbar cushion and multiple adjustments to help maintain a healthy seated posture.

## Features

### Comfort & Materials
- TEMPUR-Material™ seat cushion for conforming comfort
- Exclusive TEMPUR® lumbar cushion made with adaptive TEMPUR-Material™
- Even weight distribution over the seat surface
- Pressure relief by conforming to your body’s unique shape
- Responsive material that reacts to body temperature and weight, then returns to its original shape when you stand up

### Ergonomics & Adjustability
- Built-in ergonomic design to promote a healthy seated position
- Height adjustment via lever, with up to 4" of travel
- Adjustable tilt of the breathable mesh back, up to 7°
- Tilt tension control to adjust how easily the chair reclines
- Tilt lock to fix the backrest angle when desired
- 360° swivel base
- Adjustable armrests
- Substantial lumbar support for the lower back

### Design & Aesthetics
- Breathable mesh back for airflow
- TEMPUR® seat and lumbar cushions integrated into the chair design
- Multiple cushion color options to complement home or office decor
- Premium wheels for smooth rolling on most office floors

### Assembly & Environmental
- Easy assembly with step-by-step instructions
- Tested and evaluated by UL to meet stringent chemical emissions limits
