### SitOnIt Novo WFH High Back Mesh Task Chair

![SitOnIt Logo](/path/to/sitonit/logo.png)

#### Overview
The **SitOnIt Novo High Back Mesh Task Chair** is part of the Work From Home series, specifically designed to offer ergonomic support for remote workers. Featuring a milestone seat and electric blue mesh, this chair is engineered for both comfort and style.

#### Features
- **High Back Design:** Provides extended support for the upper back and shoulders.
- **Mesh Backrest:** Offers breathability and promotes airflow for long hours of sitting.
- **Milestone Seat:** Designed for comfort with a contoured shape.
- **Ergonomic Support:** Tailored to enhance posture and reduce strain.
- **Color Options Available:**
  - Electric Blue Mesh
  - Nickel Mesh
  - Black Mesh
- **Frame Options:**
  - White Frame
  - Black Frame
