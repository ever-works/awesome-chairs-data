# Branch Softside Office Chair (High-Back)

A high-back, cushioned office chair from Branch designed to prioritize softness and comfort for all-day computer work.

---

## Features

- **High-back design** for upper-back and shoulder support
- **Soft, cushioned seat and backrest** to emphasize comfort over firm, technical ergonomics
- **Boxed-in armrests** for a more enclosed, lounge-like feel
- **Narrower seat profile**, which may not be ideal for wider users or those who prefer extra seat width
- **Modern, colorful aesthetic** suitable for contemporary home offices and workspaces
- **Intended for full workday use** at a computer or desk
