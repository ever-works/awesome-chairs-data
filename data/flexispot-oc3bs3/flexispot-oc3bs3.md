# Flexispot OC3/BS3 Ergonomic Office Chair

![Flexispot OC3/BS3](https://www.pcgamer.com/flexispot-oc3-bs3-image1.jpg)

## Overview
The **Flexispot OC3/BS3** is an ergonomic office chair designed to offer comfort and affordability for both office workers and gamers. It features a breathable mesh back and a variety of adjustable settings, making it a practical choice for those who spend extended periods sitting.

## Features
- **Ergonomic Design:** Supports a healthy posture with its ergonomic build.
- **Adjustable Settings:** Includes adjustable armrests, seat height, and tilt tension to cater to individual comfort needs.
- **Breathable Mesh Back:** Ensures proper airflow, keeping you cool during long hours.
- **Easy Assembly:** Comes with a straightforward setup process.
- **Coat Hanger:** Equipped with a convenient hanger for jackets or coats.

## Conclusion
The Flexispot OC3/BS3 is a well-designed chair offering many features beneficial to those who require an ergonomic seating solution. While it presents a good balance of design and affordability, some users may find the seat comfort lacking for prolonged use. Nonetheless, its adjustable features and breathable design make it a strong contender in the ergonomic chair market.
