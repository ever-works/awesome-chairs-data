## Overview
The **Haworth Soji** is a premium ergonomic office chair designed to enhance comfort and support for individuals who spend extended hours seated. It is crafted for versatility, accommodating various body types with its adjustable features.

## Features
- **Ergonomic Design**: Engineered to promote healthy posture and alleviate discomfort during prolonged sitting.
- **Adjustable Lumbar Support**: Offers customizable lumbar support to maintain proper back alignment.
- **4-Dimensional Armrests**: Allows adjustments in height, width, depth, and pivot for personalized comfort.
- **Seat Depth Adjustment**: Adapts to different leg lengths, enhancing seating comfort.
- **Waterfall Seat Edge**: Reduces pressure on thighs and knees, promoting better circulation.
- **Solid Warranty and Return Policy**: Provides peace of mind with robust warranty coverage and free returns.
