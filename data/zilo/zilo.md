# ZILO

**Brand:** AMQ  
**Category:** Task / Office Chair

Task chair with ergonomic adjustments and configurable styles, designed for extended computer or office work.

---

## Features

- **Configurations**
  - Available in high-back configuration
  - Available in mid-back configuration

- **Ergonomics & Comfort**
  - Adjustable arms
  - Built-in seat slider for seat depth adjustment
  - Adjustable lumbar support
  - Breathable mesh back for airflow and temperature control
  - Designed for all-day sitting support

- **Design & Finish Options**
  - Multiple aesthetic options (e.g., light grey and black finishes shown)
  - Available with polished aluminum base
  - Mesh seat option available (in addition to upholstered options)
