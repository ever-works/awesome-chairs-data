# Steelcase Office Chairs

**Category:** Chair Accessories

## Overview
Steelcase office chairs are ergonomic office and computer chairs designed for people who spend extended periods sitting at a desk or computer. Developed by Steelcase, a global workplace solutions company, these chairs focus on posture support and long-term comfort using evidence-based ergonomic design.

## Features
- Ergonomic office and computer chair designs
- Suited for extended daily computer and desk work
- Advanced posture-sensing mechanisms to support healthy sitting positions
- Automated adjustment systems that adapt to the user without extensive manual setup
- Evidence-based ergonomic design informed by workplace research

## Ideal Use Cases
- Full-time computer and desk work
- Home offices and corporate workstations
- Users who sit for long periods and need consistent posture support
