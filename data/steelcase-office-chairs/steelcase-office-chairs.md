## Steelcase Office Chairs

**Category:** Chair Accessories  
**Brand:** Steelcase  
**Website:** https://www.steelcase.com

### Overview
Steelcase Office Chairs are ergonomic seating solutions designed for people who spend extended periods working at a computer. Produced by Steelcase Inc., a global workplace solutions company, these chairs focus on posture support, automatic adjustments, and evidence-based ergonomics.

### Features
- **Ergonomic design for extended computer use** – Built specifically for users who sit at a desk or computer for long periods.
- **Advanced posture-sensing mechanisms** – Chair mechanisms detect and respond to user posture to help maintain healthier alignment while seated.
- **Automated adjustment systems** – Certain settings (such as recline or resistance) automatically adapt to the user, reducing the need for manual fine-tuning.
- **Evidence-based ergonomic development** – Design is informed by ergonomic research and workplace studies to support comfort and reduce strain.
- **Workplace-focused design** – Developed by a global workplace solutions company, intended for professional and high-use environments.
