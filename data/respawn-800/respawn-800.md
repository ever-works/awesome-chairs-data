# RESPAWN 800

## Overview
The **RESPAWN 800** is a versatile gaming rocker chair designed for gamers seeking comfort and functionality. This chair is built with a pedestal base and offers a full 360-degree swivel feature, making it a dynamic addition to any gaming setup.

## Features
- **High Back Support**: Provides enhanced comfort with extra cushioning for long gaming sessions.
- **Built-in Lumbar Support**: Ensures ergonomic seating to support your back.
- **117-degree Recline**: Allows for a customized reclining experience.
- **360-degree Swivel**: Facilitates easy movement and access to different areas of your gaming setup.
- **Flip-up Arms**: Offers versatile comfort and space adjustment.
- **Weight Activated Tilt Recline**: Adjust tension for personalized recline feedback.
- **Sturdy Pedestal Base**: Provides excellent stability while rocking.
- **Color**: Available in Gray Fabric.
