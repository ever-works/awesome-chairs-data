## Overview
The Aeron Stool is a stool-height version of Herman Miller’s Aeron chair. It is designed for high work surfaces and sit-stand computer setups, providing ergonomic support for extended periods of sitting similar to the standard Aeron chair.

## Features
- Stool-height seating configuration
- Intended for use with high work surfaces
- Suitable for sit-stand and height-adjustable computer setups
- Based on the Herman Miller Aeron chair design
- Ergonomic support aimed at extended sitting
- Mesh-style construction (inferred from product tags and Aeron family design)
