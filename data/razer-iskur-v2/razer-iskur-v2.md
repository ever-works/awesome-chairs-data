## Overview
The **Razer Iskur V2** is a top-tier gaming chair engineered for professional gamers and individuals who spend long hours seated. It combines innovative ergonomic features with premium materials to deliver unmatched comfort and durability.

## Features
- **Ergonomic Lumbar Support:** Designed to provide optimal support for the lower back, reducing strain during extended gaming sessions.
- **High-Density Foam Cushions:** Offers superior cushioning and support, conforming to the user's body shape for enhanced comfort.
- **Multi-layered Synthetic Leather Finish:** A durable and stylish covering that withstands wear and tear while providing a premium look.
- **Adaptive Lumbar Curve:** Customizable lumbar support to suit individual ergonomic needs.
- **4D Armrests:** Fully adjustable armrests that can be repositioned in four directions for personalized support.
- **152-Degree Recline:** Allows users to recline comfortably to their desired angle, promoting relaxation during breaks.
