# 9to5 Seating Ergonomic Office Chairs

## Overview
Ergonomic office and task chairs from 9to5 Seating (founded 1982, California, USA), designed for all‑day computer and desk work. The product range focuses on promoting healthy posture, supporting natural movement, and reducing fatigue for people who sit for extended periods in professional or home workspaces.
