## Secretlab Titan Evo

![Secretlab Logo](https://secretlab.co/logo.png)

### Overview
The **Secretlab Titan Evo** is a premium gaming chair designed with ergonomics and comfort as its top priorities. This chair is engineered to support gamers and professionals during long sessions, boasting a variety of features that cater to different needs and preferences.

### Features
- **Customizable Lumbar Support**: Patent-pending 4-way L-ADAPT™ Lumbar Support System for personalized back support.
- **Adjustable Armrests**: Full-Metal 4D Armrests with CloudSwap™ Replacement System for complete adjustability.
- **Magnetic Memory Foam Head Pillow**: Provides additional neck support and comfort.
- **SoftWeave™ Plus Fabric**: A breathable and durable fabric that enhances comfort.
- **NEO™ Hybrid Leatherette**: Offers the luxurious feel of real leather with enhanced durability.
- **Pebble Seat Base**: Ergonomically sculpted for optimal support and comfort.
- **Cold-Cure Foam Mix**: Medium-firm foam for balanced comfort and support.
- **Multi-tilt Mechanism**: Allows for a customizable sitting angle and posture.
- **Full-Length Backrest Recline**: Offers up to 165° of recline for relaxation.
- **High-Quality Wheelbase and Casters**: ADC12 Aluminum Wheelbase and XL PU casters for stability and smooth movement.
- **NFC Anti-Counterfeit Tag**: Ensures authenticity.
- **5-Year Extended Warranty**: Offers peace of mind and long-term reliability.

### Sizing Options
The Titan Evo is available in three sizes to accommodate various body types:
- **Small**: Suitable for individuals up to 5’6” and 200 lbs.
- **Regular**: Ideal for heights between 5’7” and 6’2” and up to 220 lbs.
- **XL**: Fits users from 5’11” to 6’9” and weights between 175 lbs and 395 lbs.
