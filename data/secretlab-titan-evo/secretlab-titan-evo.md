### **Product Overview**
The **Secretlab Titan Evo** is a premium gaming chair crafted for those who value comfort, durability, and a sleek design. Whether you're a gamer or a professional, this chair promises unmatched ergonomic support and adaptability.

### **Features**
- **Ergonomic Design**: 
  - Proprietary **pebble seat base** for optimal support.
  - Patent-pending **4-way L-ADAPT™ Lumbar Support System**.
  - Full-length backrest with **165° recline**.
- **Material Quality**:
  - **Secretlab SoftWeave™ Plus fabric** and **NEO™ Hybrid Leatherette** for superior comfort and durability.
  - **Patent-pending cold-cure foam mix** for cradling comfort.
- **Adjustability**:
  - Full-Metal **4D Armrests** with **CloudSwap™ Replacement System**.
  - **Multi-tilt Mechanism** for personalized seating.
- **Build and Durability**:
  - **ADC12 Aluminum Wheelbase** and **XL PU casters**.
  - **NFC anti-counterfeit tag** for authenticity.
- **Additional Features**:
  - **Magnetic Memory Foam Head Pillow**.
  - **Shorter, re-engineered Class 4 Hydraulics**.
  - Up to a **5-year Extended Warranty**.

### **Size Options**
The Titan Evo is available in three sizes to ensure a perfect fit for everyone:
- **Small**: Suitable for individuals ≤5’6” and <200lbs.
- **Regular**: Ideal for individuals 5’7”–6’2” and <220lbs.
- **XL**: Best for individuals 5’11”–6’9” and 175lbs–395lbs.
