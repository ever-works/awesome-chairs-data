# Triigger 350 Special Edition Gaming Chair

![Triigger 350 Special Edition](https://www.vertagear.com/images/triigger-350-special-edition-1.png)

## Overview
The **Triigger 350 Special Edition** by Vertagear is a premium gaming chair crafted to provide unparalleled comfort and durability for gamers and professionals alike. It is engineered to support up to 350 lbs and is suitable for users up to 6’6” tall, ensuring a comfortable fit for a wide range of body types.

## Features
- **Ergonomic Design**: Designed to enhance comfort during prolonged gaming or work sessions.
- **Reinforced DuPont TPEE Mesh**: This high-quality mesh offers breathability and resilience, maintaining its integrity under high temperatures and heavy use.
- **Premium Calfskin Leather**: Handcrafted with fine grain leather for a luxurious texture and added comfort.
- **Aluminum Alloy 5 Star Base**: Provides stability and durability, ensuring long-lasting use.
- **Class-4 Gas Lift**: Offers industrial-grade adjustability to set the chair at the optimal height.
- **Holtron Hub-less Lockable Casters**: 3-inch casters with all-metal ball bearings for smooth gliding and a futuristic look.
- **Class 12.9 Screws**: Extremely strong screws for enhanced safety and stability.
- **Armrest Height Adjustment**: Allows quick adjustments for optimal seating positions.
- **Armrest Recline Angle Adjustment and Lock**: Customize your recline angle with ease.
