## Secretlab MAGNUS Pro

![Secretlab Logo](https://secretlab.co/logo.png)

![Secretlab MAGNUS Pro](https://secretlab.co/images/magnus-pro-1.png)

### Overview
The **Secretlab MAGNUS Pro** is a versatile standing desk designed to enhance the ergonomic benefits associated with Secretlab chairs. It facilitates easy transitions between sitting and standing, promoting better posture and reducing the adverse effects of prolonged sitting. This desk is crafted for performance, reflecting Secretlab's dedication to ergonomic design.

### Features
- **Integrated Backlit Control Panel**: Features a custom LED control panel for a seamless appearance, with an on/off switch to lock settings.
- **Anti-Collision Detection**: Automatically halts desk movement upon detecting obstacles, ensuring user safety.
- **Powder-Coated Finish**: Provides additional insulation and durability to the desk's surface.
- **Thermal Circuit Breaker**: A fail-safe design for enhanced safety.
- **Insulated 12AWG Cables**: Encased in a custom sleeve to prevent exposure.
- **Segmented and Grounded**: Multiple grounding points for increased safety.
- **Precision Electric Height Adjustment**: Offers precise height adjustments between 25.6” - 49.2”, with three customizable presets.
- **Fully-Integrated Power Supply Column**: Concealed cable within the desk leg and built-in electrical socket for streamlined power management.
- **Full-Length Cable Management Tray**: Spans the entire desk length, allowing easy wire concealment and organization.
- **Ultra Durable Steel Chassis**: Provides robust support and longevity.
- **Modular Add-ons and Accessories**: Offers customization and expansion options for personal setups.

### Specifications
- **Height Adjustment Range**: 25.6” - 49.2”
- **Maximum Weight Capacity**: 265 lbs
- **Color Options**: Black, Pure White
