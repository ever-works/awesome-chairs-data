## Description
The **Liberty Task Chair** by **Humanscale** is designed to seamlessly integrate into modern workspaces with its elegant and ergonomic design. It features a unique form-sensing mesh technology that adapts to your body's contours, providing customized lumbar support without the need for manual adjustments. This chair is ideal for those seeking comfort and functionality in their office environment.

## Features
- **Form-Sensing Mesh Technology**: Automatically adjusts to provide personalized lumbar support.
- **Automatic Recline Mechanism**: Allows for effortless movement and maintains comfort during long hours of use.
- **Sleek Design**: Combines aesthetics with functionality, suitable for contemporary office settings.
- **Brand**: Humanscale, known for its ergonomic office solutions.
