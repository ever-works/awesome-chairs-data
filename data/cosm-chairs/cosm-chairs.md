# Cosm Chairs

- **Brand:** Herman Miller  
- **Category:** Ergonomic Office Chairs  
- **Designer:** Studio 7.5  
- **Use Case:** High-end ergonomic task chair for long hours at a computer / all-day sitting

## Description
Cosm is a family of premium ergonomic office chairs designed to provide continuous support during long periods of computer or desk work. The chair automatically responds to the user’s body, movement, and posture to maintain natural balance and comfort with minimal manual adjustment.

## Features
- **Automatic Ergonomic Response**  
  - Automatically adjusts tilt and support to the user’s body and movements.  
  - Instantly responds to posture changes for continuous comfort.

- **Support for Long Sessions**  
  - Designed for prolonged, all-day sitting.  
  - Provides consistent ergonomic support while working at a computer.

- **Flexible Frame & Structure**  
  - Flexible frame designed to move with the user.  
  - Aims to distribute support evenly while allowing natural movement.

- **Multiple Back Options (Family of Chairs)**  
  - Available in low-back, mid-back, and high-back variants.  
  - Allows selection based on support preference and task type.

- **Arm Options**  
  - Available with distinctive “Leaf” arms and other arm configurations (per imagery and product family).  
  - Arm styles designed to complement ergonomic posture and comfort.

- **Aesthetic & Finish Options**  
  - Shown in multiple colorways, including:  
    - Graphite gray  
    - Mineral gray (with white frame)  
    - Nightfall navy blue  
    - Glacier light blue  
    - Canyon red (low-back)  
  - Contemporary visual design suitable for home offices, small businesses, and workplace environments.

- **Use Environments**  
  - Home offices and small businesses (customizable and purchasable online).  
  - Larger workplaces and commercial environments (supports specification by architects and designers).

- **Design & Integration Resources**  
  - Dimensions, materials, and 3D models available for design/specification workflows (via the manufacturer’s tools section).  
  - Resources intended for architects and designers to integrate Cosm into workplace layouts.
