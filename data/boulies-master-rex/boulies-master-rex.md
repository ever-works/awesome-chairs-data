# Boulies Master Rex

**Category:** Chair & Accessories  
**Brand:** boulies  
**Type:** Premium ergonomic computer / gaming chair (hybrid gaming & office)  
**Source:** <https://boulies.com/products/master-rex>

## Overview
The Boulies Master Rex is a high-back ergonomic computer and gaming chair designed for long sitting sessions (up to roughly a full workday). It combines a deep, wide seat, integrated leg rest, and extensive adjustability to function as both a gaming chair and an office task chair.

## Features

### Ergonomics & Support
- **High-back ergonomic profile** for full back and shoulder support.
- **Customizable integrated lumbar support** with vertical (up/down) adjustment to align with the spine’s natural curve.
- **Tailored round-edge seat cushion design**:
  - Rounded seat-front and edges instead of sharp corners.
  - Shaped to follow body contours to reduce pressure on hips and thighs.
  - Provides a wider seating area to support varied sitting postures.
- **Integrated soft leg rest**:
  - Built-in extendable leg rest to support the thighs and lower legs.
  - Intended to reduce pressure on the legs during recline.

### Recline, Tilt & Rocking
- **Backrest recline range:** approx. **95° to 165°** (upright to deep recline) for working, casual use, or resting.
- **Enhanced tilt mechanism**:
  - Allows **up to 15° rocking** when unlocked.
  - Can be locked in a tilted-back position.
  - Designed for stable, smooth tilt without wobble.

### Armrests
- **Aluminum-constructed 4D armrests**:
  - Internal structure made from **high-grade aluminum alloy** for increased durability and smoother adjustment.
  - 4D adjustability (height, depth, width, and pivot/angle – implied by “4D”).
  - Redesigned arm pads/pods with a softer top surface for forearm and elbow comfort.

### Materials & Upholstery Options
- **Boulies Ultraflex PU leather**:
  - Synthetic PU leather formulated for durability and a smooth, soft touch.
  - Inspired by microfiber suede upholstery used in sports cars.
  - Intended for a balance of comfort and long-term wear resistance.
- **Boulies water-repellent fabric**:
  - Fabric upholstery with a soft hand-feel and enhanced durability.
  - **Water-repellent treatment** to reduce absorption from spills (hydrophobic effect can diminish with time/use).
  - **Breathable construction** to help manage heat and moisture.

### Design & Use Case
- **Hybrid gaming/office design**: suitable for both workstation and gaming setups.
- **Modern, high-back, gaming-style silhouette** with deep cushioning.
- Built for **extended sitting periods** (up to around 8 hours per day, depending on use and user).
