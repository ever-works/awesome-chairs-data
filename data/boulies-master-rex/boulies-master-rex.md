# Boulies Master Rex

Flagship premium computer and gaming chair designed for up to 8‑hour sessions, combining luxury‑grade materials with ergonomic support, deep cushioning, and durability. Suitable as a hybrid gaming and office chair.

[View on manufacturer site](https://boulies.com/products/master-rex)

## Features
- **Ergonomic design for work and leisure** – Built for long sitting sessions with posture support for both productivity and relaxation.
- **Adjustable, integrated lumbar support**  
  - Customizable lumbar system with vertical (up/down) adjustment.  
  - Allows alignment to the spine’s natural curve for more personalized lower‑back support.
- **Recline and rocking**  
  - Backrest recline range: **95° to 165°** to accommodate upright work, relaxed lounging, or near‑recline rest.  
  - Robust tilt mechanism allows **smooth rocking** or a **locked tilt‑back** position.
- **Enhanced tilt mechanism**  
  - Supports approximately **15° rocking**.  
  - Designed with improved stability and durability for secure leaning and reclining.
- **Tailored round‑edge seat cushion**  
  - Rounded front and side edges instead of sharp corners to better follow body contours.  
  - Aims to reduce pressure on hips and thighs.  
  - **Wider seating area** to accommodate varied sitting postures.
- **Aluminum‑constructed 4D armrests**  
  - Internal structure made from high‑grade aluminum alloy for increased strength and longevity.  
  - Smoother operation vs. standard plastic mechanisms.  
  - Redesigned, **softer arm pads** to improve elbow and forearm comfort.  
  - 4D adjustment (directional adjustability implied by “4D” armrests).
- **Integrated soft leg rest**  
  - Built‑in, extendable leg rest to support thighs and lower legs.  
  - Intended to reduce pressure and enhance relaxation when reclining.  
  - Can be activated and stowed as needed.

## Materials & Upholstery Options
- **Boulies Ultraflex PU leather**  
  - Synthetic leather developed for a soft, smooth, “buttery” feel.  
  - Inspired by microfiber suede used in sports‑car interiors.  
  - Emphasizes a balance of durability and comfort.
- **Boulies water‑repellent fabric**  
  - Fabric upholstery option designed for durability with a soft touch.  
  - **Breathable** to help manage heat and moisture.  
  - **Water‑repellent treatment** to resist spills and reduce liquid absorption.  
  - Hydrophobic effect gradually diminishes over time; rate depends on use and care.

## Intended Use
- Extended computer work sessions (home office or professional use).
- Gaming and general entertainment. 
- Users seeking a single chair for both office and gaming setups.
